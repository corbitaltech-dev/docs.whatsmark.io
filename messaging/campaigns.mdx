A campaign sends the same message to many contacts at once. Use it for an offer, an announcement, or a follow-up to a group of people.

<Note>
Before you start, you need a **[connected channel](/core-setup/channels/whatsapp)** to send from and **[contacts](/contacts/managing-contacts)** to send to.
</Note>

## Campaign statuses

The status shows the current stage of the campaign.

| Status |  It means |
| --- | --- |
| **Draft** | Made but not sent yet. You can still edit it, and you send it. |
| **Queued** | Scheduled and waiting for its send time. |
| **Preparing** | Getting the list of recipients ready. This lasts a moment. |
| **Sending** | Messages are going out right now. |
| **Paused** | Sending is on hold. Resume it to carry on. |
| **Completed** | Every recipient has been handled. |
| **Failed** | The campaign could not run, often because no one was eligible to receive it. |
| **Cancelled** | You stopped it. Anyone not yet sent to will not be sent to. |

## The two campaign types

You pick one of two types when you create a campaign.

### Message campaign

A message you create yourself using text, images, videos, or buttons. You can send it only to people who have messaged you in the [last 24 hours](/getting-started/concepts#two-whatsapp-rules-that-shape-everything). No approved template is needed.
### Template campaign

Sends a **Meta-approved [template](/messaging/whatsapp-templates)**. It can reach **any** contact on the channel. You need an approved template first. Use it for offers, announcements, and reminders.

## Creating a campaign

Campaigns are built in four steps. A live preview of your message shows on the right as you go.

<Steps>
<Step title="Basics">
  <BrowserFrame
    src="/messaging/img/campaign/campaign-basics.png"
    url="whatsmark.io/campaigns/create"
    alt="The campaign basics step with a name field, a choice between Template and Message campaign, a channel picker, and a template picker"
    caption="Image shows the basics step of the wizard."
  />

  - **Campaign name** — a name only you see, like *Diwali sale 2026*.
  - **Campaign type** — Select type of campaign.
  - **Channel** — which of your channel to send from.
  - **Template** — for a template campaign, select the approved template to send.
</Step>

<Step title="Audience">
  Select the exact contacts who should receive the campaign.

  <BrowserFrame
    src="/messaging/img/campaign/campaign-audience.png"
    url="whatsmark.io/campaigns/create"
    alt="The audience step with filters for status, source, group and type, a search box, and a contact list with checkboxes and a Select all filtered option"
    caption="Image shows the audience step of the wizard."
  />

  Filter the list with the **status**, **source**, **group** and **type** filters, or search by name or phone. Select the contacts you want, or use **Select all filtered** to tick everyone in the current filtered list. You need at least one contact to continue.
</Step>

<Step title="Content">
  Write what you want to send.

  <BrowserFrame
    src="/messaging/img/campaign/campaign-content.png"
    url="whatsmark.io/campaigns/create"
    alt="The content step showing a message body field with merge field support and options to add buttons or media"
    caption="Image shows the content step of the wizard."
  />

  - **Template campaign** — fill in the template's variables. If the template has a media header, upload the image or file here.
  - **Message campaign** — write a **header** (optional), a **body**, and a **footer** (optional). You can also add up to three reply **buttons**, a single **call-to-action** link, or attach an image, video or document.

  Type `@` in any text field to add a **merge field** like the contact's name. See [Merge Fields](/messaging/merge-fields).
</Step>

<Step title="Schedule">
  Choose when to send it.

  <BrowserFrame
    src="/messaging/img/campaign/campaign-schedule.png"
    url="whatsmark.io/campaigns/create"
    alt="The schedule step with a choice between Send now and Schedule for later, a date and time picker, and a summary of the campaign"
    caption="Image shows the schedule step of the wizard."
  />

  - **Send now** — the campaign is sent as soon as you create it.
  - **Schedule for later** — select a date and time. Confirm your [timezone](/settings/general) first, so it sends at the right time.

  <Warning>
  Once a campaign start sending you cannot edit it. Check the summary before you send.
  </Warning>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Messenger campaigns

If you have a Messenger channel connected, you can pick it as the channel type in [step 1](#creating-a-campaign).

Messenger has one important difference: **both** message and template campaigns can only reach contacts who messaged your Page in the **last 7 days**.

## Campaign reports

Open any campaign to see its report.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/messaging/img/campaign/campaign-detail.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/campaigns"
  alt="A campaign report with cards for total sent, delivered, not received, read and failed, an engagement breakdown chart, and a preview of the message"
  caption="Image shows a campaign report with its delivery figures."
/>

The report shows how the campaign is doing:

- **Sent**, **Delivered**, **Not received**, **Read** and **Failed** counts, each with a percentage.
- An **engagement breakdown** for delivered, read, replied and button clicks.
- A preview of the message exactly as people receive it.

Depending on its status, you can **Pause**, **Resume** or **Cancel** a campaign, or **Delete** it once it is finished.

Two more actions:

- **Resend Failed** — send again only to the contacts whose message failed.
- **New Campaign for Replied** — start a fresh campaign for the contacts who replied. It opens as a draft for you to edit.

Click **Recipients** to see every contact in the campaign, filter them by status (pending, sent, delivered, read, replied, failed), and read the error on any that failed.

## Campaign limits

Your plan includes a number of **message campaigns** and **template campaigns** each month, counted separately.
