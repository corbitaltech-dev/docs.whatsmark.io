**Chat** is where your team actually talks to customers — a unified inbox for every WhatsApp and Messenger conversation across all your connected channels.

## The inbox layout

Chat is split into four panes:

1. **Sidebar** — filters: **All**, **Mine**, **Unassigned**, per-status, per-channel, and **Team Inboxes**, each with a live unread counter.
2. **Conversation list** — every conversation matching the current filter, searchable by contact.
3. **Message thread** — the open conversation, with message search within the thread.
4. **Contact panel** — the contact's details, media gallery, and notes, without leaving the chat.

Updates appear instantly — new messages, delivery ticks, assignment changes, and counters all update live without needing to refresh the page.

## Sending a message

The composer supports:

- **Text**
- **Media** — images, video, audio, and documents, with upload progress
- **Location** pins
- **Contact cards**
- **Canned replies** — a searchable picker of pre-written responses
- **Reply-to** — quote a specific earlier message

<Warning>
WhatsApp only allows free-form messages within a **24-hour window** after the customer's last message. Once that window closes, the composer shows a **"Send template"** prompt instead — see [Initiate Chat](/contacts/managing-contacts#row-actions) for sending an approved template to restart the conversation. Messenger conversations don't have this restriction.
</Warning>

## AI-assisted replies

Click the **Sparkles** button in the composer to:

- **Improve**, **Fix Grammar**, **Make Formal**, **Make Casual**, or **Shorten** your drafted message.
- **Translate** it into any language from your configured list.
- Apply one of your own **Custom AI Prompts**.

After a rewrite, an **Undo / Regenerate** bar lets you revert or try again, and a token-usage meter shows how much of your monthly AI quota you're using.

<Note>
AI assist is gated by your plan (`ai_chat_assist` as the master switch, `ai_tokens_monthly` for usage, `ai_prompts` for how many custom prompts you can save). See [AI Features](/automation/ai-features) for the full picture.
</Note>

## Managing a conversation

- **Assign** a conversation to one or more team members, or to a **Team Inbox**.
- **Mark as read**, **clear** the message history, or **delete** the conversation entirely.
- **Delete a single message** — you (or a teammate) can **restore** it within a 2-minute window.
- **Run an automation flow** manually against the open conversation, or send a **WhatsApp Flow** interactively — see [Automation Flows](/automation/chatbot-flows) and [WhatsApp Flows](/automation/whatsapp-flows).

<Tip>
Assigning conversations and running automation flows from chat both respect your workspace's [roles and permissions](/core-setup/team-and-roles) — a team member needs the right permission to see these actions.
</Tip>

## Team Inboxes

Beyond assigning conversations to individuals, you can group team members into **Team Inboxes** (e.g. "Sales", "Support"):

<Steps>
<Step title="Create a Team Inbox">
  Give it a name from the Team Inboxes management screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Add members">
  Add the team members who should see conversations routed to this inbox.
</Step>

<Step title="Assign conversations to it">
  From any conversation, assign it to the Team Inbox instead of (or alongside) an individual — everyone in that inbox sees it in their sidebar.
</Step>
</Steps>
