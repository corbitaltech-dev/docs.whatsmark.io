**Chat** is where your team actually talks to customers — a unified inbox for every WhatsApp and Messenger conversation across all your connected channels.

## The inbox layout

Chat is split into four panes:

1. **Sidebar** — filters: **All**, **Mine**, **Unassigned**, per-status, per-channel, and **Team Inboxes**, each with a live unread counter.
2. **Conversation list** — every conversation matching the current filter, searchable by contact.
3. **Message thread** — the open conversation, with message search within the thread.
4. **Contact panel** — the contact's details, media gallery, and notes, without leaving the chat.

The contact panel isn't read-only — you can **edit the contact's details directly from here** (name, status, labels, and other fields), the same as editing them from the [Contacts](/contacts/managing-contacts) page, and add or review [notes](/contacts/managing-contacts#notes) without switching pages.

Updates appear instantly — new messages, delivery ticks, assignment changes, and counters all update live without needing to refresh the page. An **online indicator** shows on a contact's avatar when they're currently active.

## Starting a new chat

You don't have to wait for a customer to message first — click **New Chat** to search your existing contacts and open a conversation directly from Chat:

- If the contact already has an open conversation (within the 24-hour window), it opens immediately.
- If their window is closed, or they're reachable on more than one platform, you'll be guided through a **channel picker** and then the [Initiate Chat](/contacts/managing-contacts#row-actions) template flow instead.

## Browsing message history

Use the **date range picker** above the message thread to jump to an earlier point in a conversation's history.

<Note>
How far back you can go is limited by your plan's chat history retention (`chat_history_days`). Dates outside your plan's window are disabled in the picker, with a link to [Billing](/billing/plans-and-subscription) if you need to look further back.
</Note>

## Sending a message

The composer supports:

- **Text**
- **Media** — images, video, audio, and documents, with upload progress
- **Location** pins
- **Contact cards**
- **Canned replies** — a searchable picker of pre-written responses
- **Reply-to** — quote a specific earlier message

<Warning>
WhatsApp only allows free-form messages within a **24-hour window** after the customer's last message. A live countdown above the composer shows exactly how much time is left. Once that window closes, the composer shows a **"Send template"** prompt instead — see [Initiate Chat](/contacts/managing-contacts#row-actions) for sending an approved template to restart the conversation. Messenger conversations don't have this restriction, so no countdown appears for them.
</Warning>

## AI-assisted replies

Click the **Sparkles** button in the composer to:

- **Improve**, **Fix Grammar**, **Make Formal**, **Make Casual**, or **Shorten** your drafted message.
- **Translate** it into any language from your configured list.
- Apply one of your own **Custom AI Prompts**.

After a rewrite, an **Undo / Regenerate** bar lets you revert or try again, and a token-usage meter shows how much of your monthly AI quota you're using.

<Note>
AI assist is gated by your plan. See [AI Features](/automation/ai-features) for the full picture.
</Note>

## Managing a conversation

- **Assign** a conversation to one or more team members, or to a **Team Inbox**.
- **Mark as read**, **clear** the message history, or **delete** the conversation entirely.
- **Delete a single message** — you (or a teammate) can **restore** it within a 2-minute window.
- **Run an automation flow** manually against the open conversation, or send a **WhatsApp Flow** interactively — see [Automation Flows](/automation/chatbot-flows) and [WhatsApp Flows](/automation/whatsapp-flows).

<Tip>
Assigning conversations and running automation flows from chat both respect your workspace's [roles and permissions](/core-setup/team-and-roles) — a team member needs the right permission to see these actions.
</Tip>

## Canned Replies

**Canned Replies** are pre-written responses your team can insert into the composer with one click, instead of retyping the same answer over and over. Manage the list from **Setup → Canned Replies**:

- **Create**, **edit**, and **delete** a canned reply.
- **Drag to reorder** — the order here is the order they appear in the composer's picker.

<Warning>
The number of canned replies you can save is limited by your plan. Free-plan workspaces can't create any; Professional and Reseller plans are unlimited.
</Warning>

## Team Inboxes

Beyond assigning conversations to individuals, you can group team members into **Team Inboxes** (e.g. "Sales", "Support"):

<Steps>
<Step title="Create a Team Inbox">
  Give it a name from the Team Inboxes management screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Add members">
  Add the team members who should see conversations routed to this inbox.
</Step>

<Step title="Assign conversations to it">
  From any conversation, assign it to the Team Inbox instead of (or alongside) an individual — everyone in that inbox sees it in their sidebar.
</Step>
</Steps>
