The **Notifications** bell keeps you updated on important events without needing to check the Activity Log — things like a [template](/messaging/whatsapp-templates) being approved or rejected, or a channel being suspended.

## Viewing notifications

Click the bell icon anywhere in the dashboard for a quick dropdown of recent notifications, or go to the full **Notifications** page to see everything at once.

## Managing notifications

- **Mark as read** — a single notification, or all of them at once.
- **Delete** — a single notification, or all of them at once.
- **Bulk actions** — select multiple notifications and mark them read or delete them together.
