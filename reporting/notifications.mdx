The **Notifications** bell keeps you updated on important events without needing to check the Activity Log — things like a [template](/messaging/whatsapp-templates) being approved or rejected by Meta, a payment succeeding or failing, or a bulk action finishing in the background.

## Viewing notifications

Click the bell icon anywhere in the dashboard for a quick dropdown of recent notifications, or go to the full **Notifications** page to see everything at once. Each row shows the notification **type**, a title and message, whether it's **Unread**, and when it arrived. Sort by any column, or use **Filters** and search to narrow the list.

## What you'll be notified about

Notifications fall into a few groups:

- **Templates** — Template Status Updated (approved or rejected by Meta) and Template Category Changed.
- **Billing & payments** — Payment Received, Payment Failed, Subscription Updated, Offline Payment Approved / Rejected, and Trial Ending.
- **Account** — Welcome, Account Activated, Account Suspended, and Account On Hold.
- **Background jobs** — Bulk Action Completed, so you know when a long-running bulk contact action has finished, and Contact Downgrade Skipped.
- **Messages from the platform team** — sent by your provider's admin.

## Managing notifications

- **Mark as read** — a single notification, or all of them at once.
- **Delete** — a single notification, or all of them at once.
- **Bulk actions** — select multiple notifications and mark them read or delete them together.
