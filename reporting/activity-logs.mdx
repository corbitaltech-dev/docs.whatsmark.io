WhatsMark.io keeps two separate logs: a general **Activity Log** covering everything that happens in your workspace, and a **WhatsApp Activity Log** specifically for every outbound WhatsApp message the platform sends.

## Activity Log

The Activity Log is a filterable audit trail of every meaningful action taken in your workspace — filter by **event type**, **date range** and by **user** (the person who performed the action).

Quick tabs across the top narrow the list to **All**, **Created**, **Updated**, or **Deleted** without opening the filter panel.

You can also:

- **Delete** an individual log entry (unless it's protected from deletion).
- **Bulk-delete** selected entries.
- **Clear** the entire log.

<Tip>
Use the Activity Log when you need to answer "who changed this, and when?" — role changes, deleted contacts, settings updates, and more all show up here.
</Tip>

## WhatsApp Activity Log

This log is narrower but deeper: every outbound WhatsApp message your platform has sent on your behalf, across **every send source** —

- [Initiate Chat](/contacts/managing-contacts#row-actions)
- [Campaigns](/messaging/campaigns)
- [Automation flows](/automation/chatbot-flows) (Bot Flow)
- Message Bot
- Inbox replies (from [Live Chat](/messaging/live-chat))

<BrowserFrame
  src="/reporting/img/whatsapp-activity-log.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/whatsapp-activity-log"
  alt="The WhatsApp Activity Log page headed 'Every outbound message sent on your behalf', with an All / Success / Errors tab row and a table of sent messages showing a category badge such as Initiate Chat or Campaign, the recipient name, the template name, a delivery status badge, any error, relation type, and the created timestamp"
  caption="The Category column shows which send source produced each message — Initiate Chat, Campaign, and so on."
/>

Filter by category, or use the **All / Success / Errors** tabs to jump straight to anything that failed. Open any row to see the full technical detail of what was sent to WhatsApp and what came back. If a message didn't arrive, or arrived looking wrong, this is where you'll find out why — and it's the detail our support team will ask for if you need a hand.

Like the general Activity Log, you can clear the log, bulk-delete entries, or delete a single entry.

<Note>
The WhatsApp Activity Log is about *what was sent and how Meta responded* — for delivery/read analytics on a specific message, see the sending feature itself (Campaign analytics, Template analytics, etc.).
</Note>
