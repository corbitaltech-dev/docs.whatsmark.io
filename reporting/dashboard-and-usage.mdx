Your **Dashboard** is the home screen of your workspace, and the **Feature Usage** page is where you check exactly how much of your plan you've used.

<Info>
The Dashboard basics are already covered in [Getting Started](/getting-started/overview#your-dashboard) — this page goes deeper into the charts and the dedicated usage page.
</Info>

## Dashboard widgets

- A **greeting card** with your avatar and date, plus **Open Inbox** and **New Campaign** shortcuts.
- A rotating **banner carousel** — promotional messages your workspace if admin has configured.
- A collapsible **Usage & Limits** panel — Cards for each plan feature that has a limit or quota, with links to jump straight to that feature.
- An **Upcoming Campaigns** panel.
- **Charts**, each with its own date-range selector (Today / 7d / 15d / 30d / 1y):
  - Messages over time
  - New contacts trend
  - Contact lifecycle breakdown (leads / customers / guests)
  - Contact source breakdown
  - Per-channel inbound/outbound breakdown
  - Most-engaged chats
  - Unreplied chats

A global **Refresh Data** button updates every widget and chart in place, without a full page reload.

### Greeting and Usage & Limits

The **Usage & Limits** panel summarises your plan at a glance — a **Tracked** count, an **Unlimited** count, a **Highest active usage** callout that names the feature closest to its cap, and a card per feature showing what's used against the limit. **View all** opens the full [Feature Usage](#feature-usage-page) page.

<Tip>
Cards marked **Period quota** (like AI Usage Tokens or Broadcast Campaigns) reset each billing period. Cards marked **Plan limit** count your total and don't reset.
</Tip>

### Messages and contacts charts

The first chart row covers volume and growth: **Messages** sent vs received over time, **Leads & Customers** as a lifecycle breakdown with a conversion percentage, and **New contacts by source**.

### Channels and message sources

**Channels** breaks messages down per connected channel with inbound and outbound counts, and **Sent messages by source** shows what initiated your outbound traffic — Templates, Direct chat, or Flows.

### Most-engaged and unreplied chats

Two conversation lists sit at the bottom of the Dashboard. **Most-engaged chats** ranks conversations by message count, and **Waiting on reply** lists chats where the contact spoke last. Each row links straight into [Chat](/messaging/live-chat).

  src="/reporting/img/dashboard/dashboard-engaged-unreplied-chats.png"

<Tip>
Use **Waiting on reply** as a daily to-do list — it surfaces contacts who messaged you and haven't heard back, before the 24-hour messaging window closes.
</Tip>

<Note>
If your email address isn't verified yet, a banner appears reminding you to verify it — some pages redirect back to the Dashboard until you do.
</Note>

## Feature Usage page

While the Dashboard shows a quick summary, **Feature Usage** is the full audit: every plan feature, grouped by category (AI, Messaging, Automation, Channel, Integration, API, Team), showing:

- Its **type** — boolean, limit, or quota.
- Current **usage vs. limit**, as a percentage bar.
- An **"Unlimited"** badge for any feature set to unlimited on your plan.
- A **near-limit warning** once you're close to running out.
- A direct link to [Billing](/billing/plans-and-subscription) to upgrade if you need more.

<Tip>
Check Feature Usage before a big push — like a large contact import or a large campaign send — so you're not surprised by a quota limit partway through.
</Tip>
