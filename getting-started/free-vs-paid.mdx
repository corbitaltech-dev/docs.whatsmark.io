WhatsMark.io has two self-serve plans — **Free** and **Professional** — plus a [**Reseller**](https://whatsmark.io/reseller) plan for agencies (currently waitlist-only). Every workspace runs the same product; the plan decides **how much** of it you can use and **which** advanced capabilities are unlocked.

<Info>
  Both plans are fully functional WhatsApp workspaces. **Free** is not a time-limited trial — it doesn't expire. It includes the AI assistant and AI flow generation on a small monthly allowance; what it leaves out is the e-commerce/Google integrations, webhook forwarding, and API access.
</Info>

### What happens at signup

| | Free | Professional |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Payment required | No | **Yes — before you can use the workspace** |
| After registration you land on | Dashboard | Payment screen |
| Currency choice | INR or USD (permanent) | INR or USD (permanent) |
| Contact-tier slider on pricing page | Not applicable | Yes — pick your contact volume |

<Warning>
  If you registered for **Professional** and haven't paid, WhatsMark.io shows a payment screen instead of the app. Complete checkout (Razorpay / Dodo / offline bank transfer) to unlock the workspace. Offline transfers stay on a pending-approval screen until our team approves them. See [Register](/getting-started/register#paid-plan).
</Warning>

## Pricing

**Professional** is billed monthly, quarterly, or yearly. Quarterly and yearly prices are already discounted totals — not per-month rates.

| Billing cycle | INR | USD |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | ₹799 | $9 |
| Quarterly | ₹2,397 | $27 |
| Yearly | ₹9,588 | $108 |

<Note>
  Your **currency is permanent**. Choosing INR at registration means you always pay in INR — it can't be switched to USD later, or vice versa. USD workspaces check out through **Dodo**; INR workspaces check out through **Razorpay**. Offline bank transfer is available on both.
</Note>

## Add-ons (Professional only)

Add-ons are recurring purchases that raise a specific limit without changing your plan. They're billed on the same cycle as your subscription and are only available to **Professional** workspaces.

| Add-on | What each unit gives you | Monthly INR | Monthly USD | Max units |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Extra Contacts** | +500 contacts | ₹450 | $4.50 | 10 |
| **Extra Team Member** | +1 team seat | ₹450 | $4 | 10 |
| **Extra AI Tokens** | +1,000,000 AI tokens / month | ₹1,490 | $14 | 10 |
| **Custom Domain** | Use your own domain | ₹1,000 | $10 | 1 |

<Tip>
  Quarterly and yearly add-on pricing is discounted the same way plan pricing is. Buying the same add-on again **increases the unit count** rather than creating a duplicate line item. See [Add-ons & upgrades](/billing/addons-and-upgrades).
</Tip>

## How limits behave when you hit them

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Limits vs. quotas — what's the difference?">
    A **limit** is a ceiling on how many of something exist right now (contacts, flows, team members, channels). Delete one and you free up a slot.

    A **quota** is a monthly counter that resets each billing cycle (campaigns, conversations, AI tokens, API calls, appointment bookings). Deleting doesn't refund a quota — you wait for the reset or upgrade.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I reach a limit?">
    The action is blocked with a clear message naming the feature and your current usage, and you're offered an upgrade path. Nothing already created is deleted or disabled — you simply can't add more until you free a slot, buy an add-on, or upgrade.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where do I see my current usage?">
    Your **Dashboard** shows usage cards for the headline limits, and **Billing → Feature usage** lists every feature with a used/remaining breakdown. See [Dashboard & usage](/reporting/dashboard-and-usage).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade from Free to Professional later?">
    Yes. Go to **Billing → Plans**, pick Professional, choose your contact tier and billing cycle, and check out. Everything in your workspace — contacts, chats, templates, flows — carries over untouched, and the higher limits apply immediately after payment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I downgrade and I'm over the new limits?">
    Existing data is never deleted. You keep what you have, but you can't create anything new in an over-limit category until you're back under the cap.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
