WhatsMark.io has two self-serve plans, **Free** and **Professional**, plus a [**Reseller**](https://whatsmark.io/reseller) plan for agencies.

Both plans run the same product. Professional raises every limit and adds the integrations, webhook forwarding, and API access that Free leaves out.

<Info>
  For current prices, see the [pricing page](https://whatsmark.io/pricing). You choose your plan there before you register.
</Info>

## What each plan is for

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free" icon="gift">
    Best for trying WhatsMark.io on your own, with a small contact list and light message volume. Single user.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Professional" icon="rocket">
    Best for running your business on WhatsApp — a team sharing the inbox, regular campaigns, and connected tools like Shopify.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How they compare

| | Free | Professional |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Contacts** | Small starter limit | Much higher, and expandable |
| **Team members** | Just you | Multiple seats, expandable |
| **Connected numbers** | A couple | Several |
| **Campaigns per month** | A few | Substantially more |
| **Chatbot flows** | A few | Substantially more |
| **AI usage** | Small monthly allowance | Large monthly allowance, expandable |
| **Chat history** | Recent messages | Longer retention |
| **Shopify & WooCommerce** | — | ✅ |
| **Google Sheets & Calendar** | — | ✅ |
| **Webhook forwarding** | — | ✅ |
| **REST API access** | — | ✅ |
| **Canned replies** | — | ✅ |

<Note>
  Exact numbers for every limit are shown on the [pricing page](https://whatsmark.io/pricing) and inside your workspace under **Billing → Feature usage**.
</Note>

## Good to know

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Free does not expire">
  It is a permanent plan, not a trial. You can stay on it as long as it fits your needs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Paid plans require payment before you can start">
  If you register for Professional, you'll see a payment screen instead of the dashboard until checkout is complete. Bank transfers stay pending until our team approves them. See [Register](/getting-started/register).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Your currency is permanent">
  You choose INR or USD when you register, and it cannot be changed later. Pick carefully.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Limits and quotas behave differently">
  A **limit** caps how many things you have right now — contacts, flows, team members, numbers. Delete one and the slot frees up.

  A **quota** is a monthly counter that resets each billing cycle — campaigns, conversations, AI usage. Deleting does not refund it; you wait for the reset or upgrade.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Hitting a limit never deletes anything">
  The action is blocked with a message showing your current usage, and you're offered an upgrade. Everything you have already created stays exactly as it is.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Upgrading keeps all your data">
  Go to **Billing → Plans** and choose Professional. Your contacts, chats, templates, and flows carry over untouched, and the higher limits apply as soon as payment goes through.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Add-ons raise one limit at a time">
  On Professional, you can buy extra contacts, extra team seats, extra AI usage, or a custom domain without changing plans. See [Add-ons & upgrades](/billing/addons-and-upgrades).
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Reseller plan

The **Reseller** plan is for agencies who want to sell WhatsMark.io to their own clients under their own brand. It is not yet self-serve — you can join the waitlist from the [pricing page](https://whatsmark.io/reseller).
