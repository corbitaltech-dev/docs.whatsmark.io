There's no forced setup wizard in WhatsMark.io — as soon as your workspace is ready, you land on your **Dashboard** and can use the product immediately. Everything below is self-serve: do it whenever you're ready, in whatever order makes sense for you.

<Tip>
  Just want to send something fast? The [Quickstart](/quickstart) is the shortest path to your first message. New to WhatsApp Business rules (the 24-hour window, template approval)? Start with [Key Concepts](/getting-started/concepts).
</Tip>

<Info>
  If you registered for a paid plan and haven't completed payment yet, you'll see a payment screen instead of the dashboard. See [Free vs. paid plans](/getting-started/free-vs-paid) for details.
</Info>

## Your Dashboard

Your Dashboard is the home screen of your workspace. It shows:

- Usage cards for your plan's limits (contacts, messages, Conversations, etc.)
- Message, contact, and lifecycle charts
- Upcoming scheduled campaigns
- Any promotional banners your admin has configured

From here, the sidebar gives you access to every part of the product — Contacts, Chat, Campaigns, Templates, Settings, and more.

## Connect a WhatsApp Business Account

Nothing sends or receives on WhatsApp until you connect a channel. Go to **Channels**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Channels">
    The Channels page lists every messaging account connected to your workspace, across platforms (WhatsApp, Messenger).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click **Connect Channel**, Choose the platform you want to connect (WhatsApp or Messenger) from the platform picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a connection method">
    You'll see two tabs:

    - **Embedded Signup (recommended)** — the guided route, and the one almost everyone should use.
      - A Facebook login window opens.
      - Log into your Facebook Business account.
      - Choose or create a WhatsApp Business Account and phone number.
      - Confirm, and let the window close on its own.
    - **Manual** — only for businesses that already set up WhatsApp through Meta directly and have their own connection details to enter. See [Connecting WhatsApp](/core-setup/channels/whatsapp) for what's needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check that it's connected">
    Your number appears in the Channels list marked **Active**, which means it's ready to send and receive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can connect more than one channel — for example, multiple WhatsApp numbers, or both WhatsApp and Messenger — and manage all of them from the same Channels page.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Contacts, Templates, Campaigns, and Automation pages are visible before you connect a channel, but you cannot add contacts or build templates or flows until a channel is connected. You also can't send messages until a channel is connected; WhatsMark.io will prompt you to connect one the moment you try.
</Note>

## Add contacts and start messaging

With a channel connected, this is the core workflow most workspaces settle into:

1. **Add your contacts** — import a CSV/spreadsheet or add them one by one. See [Managing Contacts](/contacts/managing-contacts) and [Importing & Exporting](/contacts/import-export).
2. **Get a WhatsApp Template approved** — required for any message sent outside the 24-hour customer service window, including every campaign broadcast. See [WhatsApp Templates](/messaging/whatsapp-templates).
3. **Start messaging** — reply to inbound conversations in [Live Chat](/messaging/live-chat), or broadcast to your contact list with a [Campaign](/messaging/campaigns).

## Invite your team

Go to **Setup → Team** to bring in other people:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Page">
    This page lists everyone with access to your workspace, plus any pending invitations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite by email">
    On the Team page, click **Invite Member**, then enter the email address of the person you want to invite and assign them a role. Click **Send Invite** to email the invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track the invitation">
    Pending invitations are listed until accepted. You can manage roles and remove access at any time from the same page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage your plan and billing

Your plan and billing details live under **Billing**, in the account menu you open by clicking your name at the bottom of the sidebar:

- View your current plan, contact tier, and billing cycle.
- Upgrade from Free to Professional, or change your contact tier/billing cycle, at any time.
- Review invoices and payment history.
- Update billing details (company name, tax ID, address) and payment method.

## Recommended order

There's no required sequence, but most workspaces follow this path:

1. Explore your **Dashboard** to get familiar with the layout.
2. **Connect a WhatsApp Business Account** so you can start messaging.
3. **Add your contacts**, imported in bulk or one at a time.
4. **Get a WhatsApp Template approved** so you're ready to broadcast.
5. **Send your first campaign**, or start replying in **Live Chat**.
6. **Invite your team** if you're not working solo.
7. Configure any remaining **Settings** (General, Billing, contact defaults) as needed.
