Creating your account is the first step to sending WhatsApp messages from WhatsMark.io. It sets
up your login and your own **workspace** — your private space in the product, at your own web
address like `acme.whatsmark.io`. It takes a couple of minutes, and you can start without a card.

<Info>
  You pick a plan *before* you fill in the sign-up form, so that's where we begin. Choosing
  **Free Forever** costs nothing and needs no payment — you can move to a paid plan whenever you
  outgrow it.
</Info>

## Step 1: Pick a plan

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/step1-pricing-plans.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/pricing"
  alt="The pricing page showing Free, Professional and Reseller plan cards"
  caption="Start on the pricing page and choose how you want to begin."
/>

Open the [pricing page](https://whatsmark.io/pricing) and choose how you'd like to start:

- **Free Forever** — no payment, no card, your workspace opens straight away.
- **Professional** — pick your options and you'll pay after the sign-up form.
- **Reseller** — for agencies, and not self-serve yet. You can [join the waitlist](https://whatsmark.io/reseller).

Click **Get Started** on your chosen plan. Your choice is carried forward automatically — the
sign-up form comes next.

<Tip>
  Not sure? Start on **Free Forever** — nothing is locked away, and upgrading later keeps
  everything you've built. [Free vs. paid plans](/getting-started/free-vs-paid) shows what each
  one includes.
</Tip>

## Step 2: Create your workspace

The sign-up form is a short two-part wizard titled **"Create your workspace."**

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/step2-account-details.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/register"
  alt="The account details form: full name, email, password, WhatsApp number and currency"
  caption="Your account details."
/>

### Your details

- **Full name** and **Email address** — use an email you can open now; we may send a link to it.
- **Password** — at least 8 characters, with an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number
  and a symbol. You'll type it twice.
- **WhatsApp number** — pick your country, then enter the number. We send a short code to it in
  the next step to confirm it's really yours, so use a number you have on you.
- **Currency** — **INR** or **USD**, for billing.

<Warning>
  **Your currency is permanent.** Whichever you pick is the one you'll always be billed in — it
  can't be switched later. Choose the one you actually want to pay in.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  In a hurry? Use **Sign up with Google** or **Facebook** above the form — one tap fills in your
  name and email and skips the password fields.
</Tip>

### Your workspace

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/workspace details.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/register"
  alt="The workspace details form with a live subdomain preview"
  caption="Name your workspace and claim its web address."
/>

- **Workspace name** — your business name, e.g. "Acme Corp". As you type, we suggest a web
  address from it.
- **Subdomain** — your workspace's web address, previewed live (e.g. `acme.whatsmark.io`) with an
  instant check that it's free. Lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens only.

  <Note>
    Pick this one thoughtfully — changing your subdomain later means contacting support, so it's
    best to get it right now. Your currency choice above is the same.
  </Note>
- Tick to agree to the **Terms of Service**, **Privacy Policy** and
  [Fair Use & Acceptable Use Policy](https://whatsmark.io/policies/fair-use-acceptable-use-policy).
  You can't continue until this is ticked.

Click **Create workspace**.

<Tip>
  Already have a workspace? Use the **"Already have a workspace? Log in"** link at the bottom of
  the form instead.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Confirm your WhatsApp number

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/step3-otp-verification.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/register"
  alt="The 6-digit WhatsApp code verification dialog"
  caption="Enter the code we send to your WhatsApp."
/>

To confirm the number is really yours, we send a **6-digit code to your WhatsApp** and a
**"Verify your WhatsApp number"** box opens.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Wait for the code">
    It usually arrives on WhatsApp within a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type it in">
    Enter the six digits. The box submits on its own once the last digit is in — there's no
    separate button to press.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Didn't arrive? Resend">
    Wait about a minute, then tap **Resend**. Only use it once the countdown finishes — repeated
    taps trigger a longer wait.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Your workspace is built

<Steps>
  <Step title="Setting up">
    You'll see a **"Setting up your workspace"** screen tick through a short checklist — creating
    the workspace, setting up its storage, creating your account, finishing touches. It usually
    takes a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    When it's done, you land in your workspace **already logged in** — no need to sign in
    separately the first time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If it's taking a while, you'll see a "this is taking longer than expected" notice — it keeps
  working in the background. If it stops, a **Try Again** button appears.
</Note>

### If you're asked to confirm your email

Some workspaces also ask you to confirm your email before you reach your dashboard. If yours
does, you'll see a **"Check your inbox"** screen: open the email we sent, click
**Verify Email Address**, and you'll land back in your workspace. No email? Use **Resend
verification email**, and check your spam folder.

### If you chose a paid plan

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/paid-plan.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/register"
  alt="The paid-plan checkout screen"
  caption="Paid plans go through a short checkout before the workspace opens."
/>

**Free Forever** workspaces open straight to the dashboard. If you picked **Professional**,
there's a short checkout first:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your plan">
    Confirm the plan and options you chose. You can still change them here before paying.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter billing details">
    Company name, billing email, tax ID, and address — these appear on your invoices and can be
    updated later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay">
    Pay online (by card, UPI, net banking and more) and your workspace opens as soon as payment
    succeeds. Or choose **bank transfer**, upload your payment proof, and you'll wait on a short
    approval screen while our team confirms it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  For how the plans differ and how billing works, see
  [Plans & subscription](/billing/plans-and-subscription).
</Info>
