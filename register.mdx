Creating a WhatsMark account means creating a **workspace** — your own isolated instance of the product, reachable at your own subdomain (e.g. `acme.whatsmark.io`). Registration starts with choosing a plan, not with a bare sign-up form.

<Info>
  If registrations are temporarily closed on a particular WhatsMark instance, you'll see a **"Registrations are temporarily closed"** notice instead of the pricing page.
</Info>

## Step 1: Choose a plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Pricing page">
    Visit the public **Pricing** page. You'll see plan cards for **Free**, **Professional**, and **Reseller**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick Free or Professional">
    - **Free** — no payment required, activated immediately.
    - **Professional** — use the contact-volume slider and the **Monthly / Quarterly / Yearly** toggle to pick the tier and billing cycle that fits your contact list size.

    Selecting **Reseller** doesn't start registration — it opens a waitlist request dialog instead, since reseller accounts are provisioned directly by a WhatsMark admin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Get Started">
    Your plan, billing cycle, and currency are carried forward automatically — you'll fill out the registration form next.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The plan you pick here determines what happens right after your workspace finishes setting up. See [Free vs. paid plans](#free-vs-paid-plans) below.
</Note>

## Step 2: Create your workspace

The registration form is a two-step wizard titled **"Create your workspace."**

### Account details

    <Card title="Full name" icon="user">
    </Card>

    <Card title="Email address" icon="envelope">
    </Card>

    <Card title="Password & Confirm password" icon="lock">
    </Card>

    <Card title="WhatsApp number" icon="phone">
    </Card>
  </CardGroup>
</Frame>

- **Full name** and **Email address** are required.
- **Password** must be at least 8 characters and include an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a symbol. You'll confirm it in a second field.
- **WhatsApp number** — enter it with the correct country code. This number is used for OTP verification (see below), so use a number you have access to.
- **Currency** — choose **INR** or **USD**. This is used for billing and pricing.

If social login (Google, GitHub, or Facebook) is enabled on your WhatsMark instance, you'll see **"Sign up with"** buttons above the form. Using one of these pre-fills your name and email and skips the password fields entirely.

As you type your email and WhatsApp number, WhatsMark checks their availability in real time, so you'll know immediately if either is already registered — you won't have to wait until final submission to find out.

### Workspace details

- **Workspace name** — e.g. "Acme Corp." As you type, a subdomain is suggested automatically.
- **Subdomain** — shown as a live preview of your workspace URL (e.g. `https://acme.whatsmark.io`), with a real-time availability check.
- **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy** — you must check this box before you can continue.
- A **CAPTCHA** may appear here if the site admin has enabled one.

Click **Create workspace** to continue.

<Tip>
  Already have a workspace? Use the **"Already have a workspace? Log in"** link at the bottom of either step instead of registering again.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Verify your WhatsApp number

If WhatsApp OTP verification is enabled on your WhatsMark instance, you won't be registered immediately after clicking **Create workspace**. Instead:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive your code">
    WhatsMark sends a 6-digit verification code to the WhatsApp number you entered in Step 2, and a **"Verify your WhatsApp number"** dialog opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    Type the 6 digits into the on-screen code boxes. The dialog submits automatically once all 6 digits are entered — there's no separate confirm button to click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend if needed">
    If you don't receive the code, use the **Resend** link. Each resend has a short cooldown (shown as a countdown, e.g. "Resend in 45s") that increases the more times you request a new code, up to a longer lockout if you retry too many times.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If your WhatsMark instance hasn't configured OTP verification, this step is skipped automatically and your workspace is created as soon as you click **Create workspace**.
</Note>

## Step 4: Workspace provisioning

Once verification succeeds (or is skipped), you'll land on a **"Setting up your workspace"** screen with a live progress checklist:

1. Creating your workspace
2. Setting up your database
3. Creating your account
4. Finishing touches

This typically finishes within a few seconds. When it's done, you'll see a short **"Your workspace is ready!"** confirmation before being redirected and automatically logged in — you don't need to log in manually right after registering.

<Warning>
  If setup fails, you'll see a **"Setup failed"** message with a **Try Again** button. If it's simply taking longer than usual, a **"This is taking longer than expected"** notice appears, but the process keeps running in the background.
</Warning>

## Email verification

If your WhatsMark instance requires email verification, you **cannot access your Dashboard, or anything else in your workspace, until you verify your email address.**
Instead of your Dashboard, you'll see a full-screen **"Check your inbox"** notice telling you to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the email">
    Open the verification email WhatsMark sent to the address you registered with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the verification link">
    Click the **Verify Email Address** button inside it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're redirected back">
    You land back in WhatsMark and your workspace is now fully accessible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you don't see the email, use the **Resend verification email** button on this screen — check your spam or junk folder if it still doesn't arrive. A **Sign out** option is also available here.


## Free vs. paid plans

What happens right after your workspace is ready depends on the plan you picked in Step 1:

| Plan | What happens after setup |
| --- | --- |
| **Free** | Activated immediately. You land straight on your **Dashboard** — no payment step. |
| **Professional** (paid) | Your workspace is created, but you'll be shown a **payment screen** before you can access anything else. |

If you chose a paid plan, you'll walk through a short checkout flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your plan">
    Confirm the plan, contact tier, and billing cycle you selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter billing details">
    Provide your company name, billing email, tax ID (e.g. GST), and address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a payment method">
    Pick from whichever gateways your WhatsMark instance has enabled — typically **Razorpay**, **Dodo**, or **offline bank transfer**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    Finish checkout through the selected gateway, or, for offline payments, submit your bank transfer proof for admin review.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you submit an offline payment, you'll see a **"Payment Proof Submitted"** status card while it's reviewed. Your dashboard stays locked behind this screen until payment is confirmed or approved.
</Info>
