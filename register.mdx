Creating a WhatsMark.io account means creating a **workspace** — your own isolated instance of the product, reachable at your own subdomain (e.g. `acme.whatsmark.io`). Registration starts with choosing a plan, not with a bare sign-up form.

<Info>
  If registrations are temporarily closed on a particular WhatsMark.io instance, you'll see a **"Registrations are temporarily closed"** notice instead of the pricing page.
</Info>

## Step 1: Choose a plan

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/step1-pricing-plans.png"
  alt="Pricing page"
  caption="Pricing page with Free, Professional, and Reseller plans"
/>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Pricing page">
    Visit the public **Pricing** page. You'll see plan cards for **Free**, **Professional**, and **Reseller**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick Free or Professional">
    - **Free** — no payment required, activated immediately.
    - **Professional** — use the contact-volume slider and the **Monthly / Quarterly / Yearly** toggle to pick the tier and billing cycle that fits your contact list size.
    - **Reseller** — coming soon. You can join the waitlist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Get Started">
    Your plan, billing cycle, and currency are carried forward automatically — you'll fill out the registration form next.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create your workspace

The registration form is a two-step wizard titled **"Create your workspace."**

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/step2-account-details.png"
  alt="Registration form"
  caption="Account details form"
/>

### Account details

- **Full name** and **Email address** are required.
- **Email address** must be valid and accessible. WhatsMark.io sends verification, so use one you can open immediately.
- **Password** must be at least 8 characters and include an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a symbol. You'll confirm it in a second field.
- **WhatsApp number** — enter it with the correct country code. This number is used for OTP verification (see below), so use a number you have access to.
- **Currency** — choose **INR** or **USD**. This is used for billing and pricing.

<Warning>
  Once you choose a currency, you **cannot change it later**. If you select **INR**, you will always pay in INR and cannot switch to USD. Similarly, if you choose **USD**, you cannot switch to INR. Choose carefully as this decision is permanent for your workspace.
</Warning>

If social login (Google or Facebook) is available, you'll see **"Sign up with"** buttons above the form. Using one of these pre-fills your name and email and skips the password fields entirely.

### Workspace details

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/workspace details.png"
  alt="Workspace details form"
  caption="Workspace details and subdomain setup"
/>

- **Workspace name** — e.g. "Acme Corp." As you type, a subdomain is suggested automatically.
- **Subdomain** — shown as a live preview of your workspace URL (e.g. `https://acme.whatsmark.io`), with a real-time availability check.
  - Your subdomain must comply with our [Fair Use & Acceptable Use Policy](https://cijagani.in/policies/fair-use-acceptable-use-policy).
  - If your subdomain violates this policy or infringes on the rights of others, your subdomain or workspace may be suspended or permanently deleted.
- **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy** — you must check this box before you can continue.
  - By registering, you agree that your subdomain and workspace content must follow the Fair Use & Acceptable Use Policy.

Click **Create workspace** to continue.

<Tip>
  Already have a workspace? Use the **"Already have a workspace? Log in"** link at the bottom of either step instead of registering again.
</Tip>

## Step 3: Verify your WhatsApp number

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/step3-otp-verification.png"
  alt="OTP verification dialog"
  caption="WhatsApp OTP verification"
/>

Complete the following WhatsApp verification steps to finish creating your workspace.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive your code">
    WhatsMark.io sends a 6-digit verification code to your WhatsApp number, and a **"Verify your WhatsApp number"** dialog opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code">
    Type the 6 digits into the on-screen code boxes. The dialog submits automatically once all 6 digits are entered — there's no separate confirm button to click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resend if needed">
    If the code does not arrive, wait about one minute and then tap **Resend**. Repeated resends can trigger a longer cooldown or temporary hold, so use the link only when the countdown has finished.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If the verification code doesn't arrive immediately, wait about one minute before tapping **Resend**.
</Note>

## Step 4: Workspace provisioning

Once verification succeeds, you'll land on a **"Setting up your workspace"** screen with a live progress checklist:

1. Creating your workspace
2. Setting up your database
3. Creating your account
4. Finishing touches

This typically finishes within a few seconds. When it's done, you'll see a short **"Your workspace is ready!"** confirmation before being redirected and automatically logged in — you don't need to log in manually right after registering.

<Warning>
  If setup fails, you'll see a **"Setup failed"** message with a **Try Again** button. If it's simply taking longer than usual, a **"This is taking longer than expected"** notice appears, but the process keeps running in the background.
</Warning>

## Email verification

You cannot access your Dashboard or anything else in your workspace until you verify your email address.
Instead of your Dashboard, you'll see a full-screen **"Check your inbox"** notice telling you to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the email">
    Open the verification email WhatsMark.io sent to the address you registered with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the verification link">
    Click the **Verify Email Address** button inside it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're redirected back">
    You land back in WhatsMark.io and your workspace is now fully accessible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you don't see the email, use the **Resend verification email** button on this screen — check your spam or junk folder if it still doesn't arrive. A **Sign out** option is also available here.


## Free plan

If you choose the **Free** plan, your workspace is activated immediately and you land straight on your **Dashboard** — no payment step.

## Paid plan

<BrowserFrame
  src="/images/paid-plan.png"
  alt="Paid plan checkout screen"
  caption="Paid plan registration and payment screen"
/>

If you choose the **Professional** plan, your workspace is created, but you'll be shown a **payment screen** before you can access anything else.

If you chose a paid plan, you'll walk through a short checkout flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your plan">
    Confirm the plan, contact tier, and billing cycle you selected. If you change your mind, click **Change my mind** to choose a different contact tier before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter billing details">
    Provide your company name, billing email, tax ID (e.g. GST), country, state, postal code, and company address.
    <Info>
      These details appear in the invoice **Bill To** section and can be updated later. If you change them after an invoice is created, the change will appear only on the next invoice — not on the previous one.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a payment method">
    Pick a payment gateway:
    - **Razorpay** — pay by cards, UPI, net banking, wallets, and other supported methods.

      You can enable **Automatic Renewal** to automatically renew your subscription using **Card AutoPay** or **UPI AutoPay**, and you can disable it later.
    - **Offline bank transfer** — pay by bank transfer instead of using the online gateway.
    - **Dodo** — if you select **USD** as your currency, Dodo will be available as the payment gateway, and the Offline Bank Transfer option will be used.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    If you pay with Razorpay, your workspace is activated immediately and you are redirected to your dashboard once payment succeeds.

    If you pay by offline transfer, submit the payment reference and proof once the transfer is complete. You will then be redirected to a pending approval screen until WhatsMark.io approves your payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  WhatsMark.io team will review your payment and activate your subscription shortly. Once approved, you'll have full access to all features.
</Info>
