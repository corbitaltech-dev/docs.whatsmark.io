This is the shortest route from a brand-new workspace to a delivered WhatsApp message. If a term here is unfamiliar, the [Key Concepts](/getting-started/concepts) page explains the WhatsApp rules behind each step.

<Info>
  If you registered for a **paid plan** and haven't paid yet, you'll see a payment screen instead of the app. Clear that first — see [Free vs. paid plans](/getting-started/free-vs-paid). **Free** workspaces skip straight to the dashboard.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect a WhatsApp channel">
    Go to **Channels → Connect Channel**, pick **WhatsApp**, and use **Embedded Signup** (recommended) to log into your Facebook Business account and pick a WhatsApp number. The channel must reach **Connected / Active** before you can send. Full details: [Connecting WhatsApp](/core-setup/channels/whatsapp).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get an approved template">
    You can't message someone new — or anyone outside the 24-hour window — without an **approved** template.

    - **Create one:** **Templates → Create**, then submit it to Meta. It stays *pending* until Meta **approves** it (usually minutes).
    - **Already have approved templates in Meta?** Use **Sync** on the Templates page to pull them in instantly — no waiting.

    See [WhatsApp Templates](/messaging/whatsapp-templates).

    <Warning>
      This is the step most likely to make you wait. A template must show **Approved** before it can be sent. Syncing existing approved templates skips the wait entirely.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a contact">
    Go to **Contacts** and add one person (or import a CSV/spreadsheet). See [Managing Contacts](/contacts/managing-contacts) and [Importing & Exporting](/contacts/import-export).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send your first message">
    Two easy first sends:

    - **To one contact:** from the **Contacts** list, use the row action **Send template** (Initiate Chat) to fire off your approved template to that person.
    - **To many:** create a **Template Campaign** under [Campaigns](/messaging/campaigns) — it broadcasts an approved template to a group of contacts.

    <Note>
      A **message campaign** (free-form broadcast) only reaches contacts with an **open 24-hour window**, so it can't be your *first* outreach to cold contacts. Start with a **template** send. See [Key Concepts](/getting-started/concepts#the-24-hour-customer-service-window).
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reply and grow">
    When customers reply, handle the conversation in [Live Chat](/messaging/live-chat), and [invite your team](/core-setup/team-and-roles) to share the inbox.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Minimum path, at a glance

**Connect a channel → get an APPROVED template (create or sync) → add a contact → Send template / Template Campaign → reply in Live Chat.**
