Instead of building from an empty canvas, click **Generate with AI** and describe what you want in plain English — for example, "qualify a lead by asking for their budget and timeline, then route to sales." WhatsMark.io proposes a starting flow matching your description, which you can then edit freely in the [Flow Builder](/automation/flow-builder).

<Warning>
AI flow generation needs a paid plan, and it draws from the same monthly AI usage allowance as [AI Chat Assist](/ai-and-more/ai-features). See [Free vs. paid](/getting-started/free-vs-paid).
</Warning>
