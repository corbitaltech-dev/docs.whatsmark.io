The **Flow Library** is a collection of pre-built flows curated by WhatsMark.io. Instead of building from an empty canvas, clone one and adjust it to your business:

- Lead Qualification
- Appointment Booking
- Customer Support
- Sales Pipeline CRM
- E-commerce Order Flow
- Recruitment / HR

Mark any flow as a **favourite** for quick access, and click **Use** to clone it into your workspace as a fully editable flow you can rename, rewire, and trigger however you like.
