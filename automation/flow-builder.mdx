The **Builder** is a canvas where you connect nodes together to shape a conversation. A flow starts from a trigger — a **keyword** or an **event** — then branches through whatever nodes you add.

## Node types

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Messaging nodes">
  Text, button, list, media, location, contact card, WhatsApp template, Messenger template, or a WhatsApp Flow send.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Input-collection nodes">
  Ask a question, or specifically ask for phone, email, address, location, or media — each waits for the contact's reply before continuing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Logic nodes">
  Delay, condition/branch, wait timer, wait-for-reply, and general logic control.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Data & integration nodes">
  API request, save response, update contact, update custom field, assign tag/label/group, Google Sheets read/write, Google Calendar event creation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Other nodes">
  Reaction, AI Assistant, sequence, connect-to-another-flow (chain flows together).
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
Google Sheets and Google Calendar nodes require connecting a [Google Account](/settings/google-accounts) first, and aren't available on the Free Forever plan.
</Note>
