**Automations** turn your WhatsApp into a chatbot that works even when you don't — it answers common questions, collects customer details, qualifies leads, and keeps conversations moving day and night. You design each conversation on a drag-and-drop canvas: when a customer sends a keyword or an event happens, your flow takes over and replies for you. Find it under **Automations** in the sidebar.


## Creating an automation

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Automations">
  Click **Automations** in the sidebar.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Create Flow">
  The button sits at the top right of the page. A form opens on the right side of the screen.

  ![New Automation form showing fields for name, channels, and description](/automation/img/automation-form.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in the details">
  - **Name** — a clear name, such as **Welcome Flow**.
  - **Channels** — the WhatsApp or Messenger accounts this automation runs on.
  - **Description** — optionally note what this flow does, so your team knows at a glance.
</Step>

<Step title="Save and build">
  Click **Save**. The flow is created as a **Draft** — open it in the [Flow Builder](/automation/flow-builder) to design the conversation, then **Active** it.
</Step>
</Steps>

Design the conversation in whichever way suits you:

## Managing your automations

<BrowserFrame
  src="/automation/img/automations-table.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/automations"
  alt="Automations page with a Flow Library button, a Create Flow button, and the All Automations table showing flows with status badges, on/off switches, and a row menu with Flow Builder, Edit, and Delete"
  caption="Image shows the Automations page with the All Automations table and the row actions menu open."
/>

The **All Automations** table lists every flow, with tabs to show **All**, **Draft**, **Active**, or **Inactive** ones.

- **Turn on or off** — flip the switch on a flow's row. Only active flows run.
- **Flow Builder** — click the menu icon at the end of a row and click **Flow Builder** to open the canvas.
- **Edit** — click the menu icon and click **Edit** to change the name, channels, or description.
- **Delete** — click the menu icon and click **Delete**. This cannot be undone.

<Note>
If an incoming message matches the trigger conditions of more than one active flow, all of them run and reply in the chat — not just one.
</Note>
