**Automations** let you build no-human-needed conversation flows — a chatbot that responds to keywords or events with a visual, drag-and-drop canvas, without writing any code.

<Warning>
The number of automation flows you can have is limited by your plan. Free-plan workspaces get 1 flow; Professional plans get 15; Reseller plans are unlimited.
</Warning>

## Building a flow

Open the **Builder** to work on a canvas (built on React Flow) where you connect nodes together. A flow is triggered by a **keyword** or an **event**, then branches through whatever nodes you add:

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Messaging nodes">
  Text, button, list, media, location, contact card, WhatsApp template, Messenger template, or a WhatsApp Flow send.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Input-collection nodes">
  Ask a question, or specifically ask for phone, email, address, location, or media — each waits for the contact's reply before continuing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Logic nodes">
  Delay, condition/branch, wait timer, wait-for-reply, and general logic control.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Data & integration nodes">
  API request, save response, update contact, update custom field, assign tag/label/group, Google Sheets read/write, Google Calendar event creation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Other nodes">
  Reaction, AI Assistant, sequence, connect-to-another-flow (chain flows together).
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
Google Sheets and Google Calendar nodes require connecting a [Google Account](/settings/google-accounts) first, and are only available on plans with `google_sheet` / `google_calendar` enabled (Professional and Reseller — not available on Free).
</Note>

## Managing flows

Create, edit, delete, or **duplicate** a flow; toggle it **active/inactive**; set its **priority** (which flow wins if more than one could match); and **export** a flow.

## Generate with AI

Instead of building from an empty canvas, click **Generate with AI** and describe what you want in plain English (for example, "qualify a lead by asking for their budget and timeline, then route to sales"). WhatsMark proposes a starting node graph matching your description, which you can then edit freely.

<Warning>
AI flow generation is gated by your plan — it's off on Free and available on Professional and Reseller plans, drawing from the same monthly AI token quota used by [AI Chat Assist](/automation/ai-features).
</Warning>
