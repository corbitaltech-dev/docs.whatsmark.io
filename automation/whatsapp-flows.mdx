**WhatsApp Flows** are Meta's interactive, form-like screens that open directly inside a WhatsApp conversation — for collecting structured information instead of relying on free text. WhatsMark gives you a visual builder for creating and managing them.

<Warning>
The number of flows you can have is limited by your plan.
</Warning>

## Building a flow

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the Builder">
  From **WhatsApp Flows**, create a new flow or open an existing one to enter the visual builder — a 3-pane layout with a screens list and field palette on one side, the screen editor in the middle, and a live phone preview on the right.
</Step>

<Step title="Add screens and fields">
  Drag fields onto a screen: **text input**, **textarea**, **phone number**, **date picker**, **dropdown**, **radio group**, **checkbox group**, or **opt-in**.
</Step>

<Step title="Publish">
  Publishing sends the flow to Meta for use. You can also **deprecate** a flow later to retire it without deleting its data.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If a flow fails to publish, click **View** in the Action column of the flows list to see the exact error Meta returned.
</Note>

<Tip>
Don't want to start from a blank canvas? Browse the **Flow Library** — pre-built Appointment Booking, Lead Capture, Survey, Support Ticket, Event Registration, and Callback Request flows — and click **Use This** to clone one straight into your workspace.
</Tip>

Other flow actions: **clone** an existing flow, toggle it **active/inactive**, and **sync** a flow (or a whole channel's flows) to pull the latest state from Meta.

## Sending a flow

A flow can be triggered four ways:

1. Directly from a [Contact](/contacts/managing-contacts#row-actions) row action.
2. Via a **FLOW button** on a WhatsApp template.
3. As part of a [Campaign](/messaging/campaigns).

## Submissions

Every time a contact completes a flow, their answers land in the **Submissions** tab for that flow:

- Browse and **export** submissions as CSV or XLSX.
- Open a submission's detail drawer to see every answer.
- **Map fields** — connect submitted answers to contact fields, [custom fields](/core-setup/contact-configuration#custom-fields), or tags, so flow data flows straight into your contact records.

## Appointment Booking flows

A special flow type generates a live, dynamic booking experience: contacts pick a date and time slot, confirm, and can later reschedule or cancel — all inside WhatsApp.

<Steps>
<Step title="Set the booking window">
  Configure slot length, capacity per slot, and how far in advance (lead time) and how far out (horizon) contacts can book, per flow.
</Step>

<Step title="Set up encryption (one-time)">
  Appointment flows use Meta's data-exchange endpoint, which requires a one-time RSA key setup per channel.
</Step>
</Steps>

Booked appointments then show up in [Appointments](/automation/appointments).
