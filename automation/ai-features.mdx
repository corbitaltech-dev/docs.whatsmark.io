WhatsMark uses AI in three places across the product — the chat composer, custom rewrite prompts, and automation flow generation — all sharing the same plan-level AI switches and token quota.

<Warning>
All AI features are off by default on the Free plan. Professional plans get AI chat assist, unlimited custom prompts, and 100,000 AI tokens per month; Reseller plans get all of the above unlimited.
</Warning>

## AI in Chat

In the [Live Chat](/messaging/live-chat) composer, click the **Sparkles** button to:

- **Improve** your drafted message, **Fix Grammar**, **Make Formal**, **Make Casual**, or **Shorten** it.
- **Translate** it into any language from your workspace's configured list.
- Apply one of your saved **Custom AI Prompts** (see below).

A token-usage meter shows live usage against your monthly quota, and an **Undo / Regenerate** bar appears after each rewrite so you can revert or try again without losing your original draft.

## Custom AI Prompts

Go to **AI Prompts** to create your own named rewrite presets — for example, "Add a friendly sign-off" or "Convert to bullet points." Each prompt is just a name plus an instruction.

<Steps>
<Step title="Create a prompt">
  Give it a name and write the instruction you want the AI to follow.
</Step>

<Step title="Reorder or toggle">
  Drag to reorder your prompts, and toggle any of them active/inactive.
</Step>

<Step title="Use it in chat">
  Your active prompts appear in the chat composer's Sparkles popover, under "Your prompts."
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The number of custom prompts you can save is limited by your plan — 0 on Free, unlimited on Professional and Reseller.
</Note>

## AI Flow Generation

Covered in full on the [Automation Flows](/automation/chatbot-flows#generate-with-ai) page — the **Generate with AI** button in the automation builder turns a plain-English description into a starting flow, drawing from the same monthly token quota as chat AI assist.
