If you need to shut down your workspace entirely, **Settings → Account** is where that happens — deliberately separate from every other settings page, and restricted to the workspace owner.

<Warning>
Only the workspace **owner** can request or cancel account closure.
</Warning>

## Requesting closure

<Steps>
<Step title="Request closure">
  From the Account page, request closure of your workspace.
</Step>

<Step title="Subscription is cancelled immediately">
  If you have an active subscription, it's cancelled right away as part of this request.
</Step>

<Step title="Deletion is scheduled">
  Your workspace isn't deleted instantly — it's scheduled for deletion after a delay, giving you a window to change your mind.
</Step>
</Steps>

Once requested, your [General settings](/core-setup/workspace-settings#general) page shows the scheduled deletion date.

## Cancelling a pending closure

If you change your mind before the scheduled deletion date, go back to the Account page and cancel the closure request — your workspace and its data are unaffected.

<Note>
Cancelling a pending closure does **not** automatically restore a cancelled subscription — you'll need to resubscribe from [Plans & Subscription](/billing/plans-and-subscription) if you want to keep using paid features.
</Note>
