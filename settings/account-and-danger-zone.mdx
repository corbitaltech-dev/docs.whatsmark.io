If you need to shut down your workspace entirely, **Settings → Account** is where that happens under **Danger Zone** — deliberately separate from every other settings page, and restricted to the workspace owner.

<Warning>
Only the workspace **owner** can request account closure.
</Warning>

## Requesting closure

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/account-danger-zone-form.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/settings/account"
  alt="Danger Zone card listing what account closure deletes, with a subdomain confirmation field, password field, and Close My Account button"
  caption="Closing your account immediately cancels your subscription and permanently deletes all data — this cannot be undone."
/>

Closing your account will:

- Immediately cancel your subscription
- Delete all users and their data
- Delete all uploaded files and media
- Delete all settings and configurations
- Permanently drop your database

<Steps>
<Step title="Type your subdomain to confirm">
  Type your workspace's subdomain exactly as shown to confirm you understand the scope of what you're deleting.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm your password">
  Enter your account password as a final safeguard.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Close My Account">
  Your subscription is cancelled immediately, and your workspace is scheduled for deletion after a delay rather than removed instantly.
</Step>
</Steps>

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/account-closed.png"
  url="whatsmark.io"
  alt="Account Closed page showing the scheduled deletion date, a note to contact support, and a Log out button"
  caption="Anyone who signs in to the workspace after closure sees this page until the scheduled deletion date."
/>

Closing the account logs everyone out immediately. Until the scheduled deletion date, this page replaces the app for all team members, showing the deletion date, a **Contact Support** link, and **Log out**.
