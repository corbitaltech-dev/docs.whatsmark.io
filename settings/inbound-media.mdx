When a customer sends you an image, video, audio note, or document over WhatsApp, Meta hosts that file only for a limited time. The **Inbound Media** setting decides whether WhatsMark saves a copy so it stays available in your conversation history.

## The setting

Go to **Settings → Inbound Media** and toggle **Download inbound media**:

- **On** — every inbound image, video, audio, and document is downloaded to your workspace and kept with the conversation, so it's still viewable after Meta's link expires.
- **Off** — inbound media isn't archived; once Meta's temporary link expires, the file may no longer be viewable.

<Warning>
Downloaded media counts toward your plan's **storage** quota. If you handle a high volume of media, keep an eye on storage usage on the [Dashboard](/reporting/dashboard-and-usage) and in the [Media Library](/media/media-library).
</Warning>
