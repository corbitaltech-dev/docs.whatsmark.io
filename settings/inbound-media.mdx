Meta's WhatsApp media URLs expire about 30 days after delivery. **Inbound Media Archival** decides whether WhatsMark.io downloads a permanent copy of everything customers send you, so it stays viewable in your conversation history after that.

## The setting

Go to **Settings → Inbound Media** and toggle **Download inbound media**:

- **On** — every inbound image, video, audio, and document is downloaded to your tenant storage and kept with the conversation, so it's still viewable after Meta's link expires.
- **Off** — new inbound media stops being downloaded. Files already archived before you turned it off are kept as-is.

<Warning>
Each archived file counts toward your plan's **storage** quota. If you handle a high volume of media, keep an eye on storage usage on the [Dashboard](/reporting/dashboard-and-usage) and in the [Media Library](/media/media-library).
</Warning>
