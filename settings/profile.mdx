Your **Profile** covers the settings that belong to *you* as a user, separate from workspace-wide settings. Open it by clicking your name at the bottom of the sidebar, then **My Profile**.

The page has a **Profile** tab and a **Password** tab, plus an **Active Sessions** card, a **Two-Factor Authentication** card, and a **Recent Activity** card showing your last actions.

## Editing your profile

On the **Profile** tab, under **Profile Information**:

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/profile-form.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/settings/profile"
  alt="Profile page showing the identity card and Profile Information form with name, WhatsApp number, and email fields"
  caption="Update your name, WhatsApp number, or avatar from the Profile tab. Email is read-only."
/>

- **Name** — your display name across the account. Required.
- **WhatsApp number** — your account's WhatsApp number.
- **Email Address** — shown for reference only. It can't be changed here — contact support if you need it updated.
- **Avatar** — click the camera icon to pick a photo from your media library or upload a new one (JPEG, PNG, or WebP, up to 5MB), or use **Remove photo** to fall back to your initials.

<Warning>
If you change the WhatsApp number, WhatsMark.io sends a 6-digit **WhatsApp OTP** to the new number before saving. Enter the code in the verification dialog to confirm the change — the update only applies once you verify it.
</Warning>

## Changing your password

On the **Password** tab, under **Change Password**:

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/password-form.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/settings/profile"
  alt="Password tab showing current password, new password, and confirm new password fields"
  caption="Switch to the Password tab to update your account password."
/>

- **Current Password** — required, unless you signed up with a social login, in which case this field is skipped.
- **New Password** and **Confirm New Password** — use **Generate strong password** for a secure suggestion, then **Use this password** to apply it.
- Click **Update Password** to save.

## Two-factor authentication

The **Two-Factor Authentication** card shows whether 2FA is currently on or off for your account.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/two-factor-card.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/settings/profile"
  alt="Two-Factor Authentication card showing 2FA status and a Manage 2FA link"
  caption="Click Manage 2FA to set up or manage two-factor authentication."
/>

Click **Manage 2FA** to open the two-factor authentication page. As a security check, you'll be asked to confirm your password first.

1. **Confirm your password**, then click **Confirm password**.
2. **Scan the QR code** with your authenticator app, or enter the setup key manually, then click **Continue**.
3. **Enter the 6-digit code** from your authenticator app and click **Confirm**.
4. **Save your recovery codes** — click **View Recovery Codes** and store them safely. Each code works once, in case you lose access to your authenticator app.

To turn 2FA off, click **Disable two-factor** on the same page.

<Tip>
Turning on two-factor authentication is one of the fastest ways to protect your account, especially for workspace owners and admins.
</Tip>

## Active sessions

The **Active Sessions** card lists every device and browser currently signed in to your account, with its browser/OS, IP address, and last-active time. Your current device is marked **This device**.

- **Log out** — sign out one specific session (for example, a shared or lost computer). Your current session can't be revoked this way.
- **Sign out all others** — sign out every session except the one you're using right now. Only shown when other sessions exist.

Both actions apply immediately, without a confirmation dialog.

<Warning>
If you suspect someone else has access to your account, change your [password](/getting-started/log-in#forgot-your-password) and click **Sign out all others** — the two together lock everyone else out immediately.
</Warning>
