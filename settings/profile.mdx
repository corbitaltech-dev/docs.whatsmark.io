Your **Profile** covers the settings that belong to *you* as a user, separate from workspace-wide settings. Open it from your account menu (click your name at the bottom of the sidebar).

## Editing your profile

- **Name** and **Number** — update the details other team members see.
- **Avatar** — upload a profile photo, or remove it to fall back to your initials.

## Active sessions

WhatsMark lists every device and browser currently signed in to your account, with its approximate location and last-active time.

- **Revoke a single session** — sign out one specific device (for example, a shared or lost computer).
- **Revoke all other sessions** — sign out everywhere except the device you're using right now.

<Tip>
If you suspect someone else has access to your account, change your [password](/getting-started/log-in#forgot-your-password) and revoke all other sessions — the two together lock everyone else out immediately.
</Tip>

## Changing your WhatsApp number

You can update the WhatsApp number on your account yourself. Because it's used for verification, the change is confirmed with a **WhatsApp OTP**: WhatsMark sends a 6-digit code to the new number and applies the change only once you enter it. If your workspace requires phone verification, you may be prompted to complete this before you can continue using the app.
