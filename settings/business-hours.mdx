## Business Hours

Go to **Settings → Business Hours** to define:

- **Weekly hours** — open/close time for each day of the week.
- **Holidays / closed dates** — specific dates marked as fully closed regardless of the weekly schedule.

<Info>
Business hours are a shared source of truth used by [Appointments](/automation/appointments) today, and by future features like out-of-hours auto-replies. Set this once and every feature that needs it reads the same schedule.
</Info>
