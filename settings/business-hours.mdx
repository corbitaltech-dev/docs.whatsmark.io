<Info>
This schedule is a single source of truth for the whole workspace. [Appointments](/automation/appointments) already uses it to offer booking slots, and upcoming features like out-of-hours auto-replies will read the same schedule — so you only need to set it once, here.
</Info>

## Weekly hours

Each day of the week has its own toggle:

- **On** — the day is open. Set an **open time** and **close time** for it.
- **Off** — the day is marked **Closed**, with no time slots offered.

Times use your workspace timezone, set on the [General](/settings/general) page.

<Tip>
Business hours don't have to be continuous 9-to-5 — each day is independent, so you can close early on some days, skip weekends, or run a split schedule by adjusting each day individually.
</Tip>

## Closed dates (holidays)

Use **Closed dates** to block specific calendar dates regardless of the weekly schedule — for example, a public holiday that falls on a normally open day.

- Pick a date and click **Add** to block it.
- Blocked dates appear as removable tags; click the **×** on a tag to unblock it.
- On a closed date, no slots are offered and the date is greyed out wherever your business hours are used for scheduling.

<Warning>
Changes to this page aren't saved automatically. Click **Save** in the bar at the bottom after making changes, or **Discard** to revert them.
</Warning>
