Go to **Settings → General** to manage your **Organization Information**:

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/general-form.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/settings/general"
  alt="Organization Information form showing organization name, subdomain, timezone, date format, and time format fields"
  caption="Update your organization's name, timezone, and date/time display format."
/>

- **Organization Name** — your organization's display name, shown throughout the dashboard and in emails.
- **Subdomain** — read-only here. Contact support if you need to change it.
- **Timezone** — the reference timezone for your workspace. Applies to campaign scheduling, business hours, appointment booking, and every timestamp shown across the system.
- **Date Format** — `Y-m-d` (2026-02-13), `d/m/Y` (13/02/2026), `m/d/Y` (02/13/2026), or `F j, Y` (February 13, 2026).
- **Time Format** — `24 Hours` or `12 Hours`.

Both control how dates and times are displayed everywhere in the system: contact, campaign, and appointment tables, chat timestamps, invoices, activity logs, and dashboard charts.

<Info>
Changes here aren't saved automatically. An unsaved-changes bar appears at the bottom once you edit a field — click **Save** to apply your changes or **Discard** to revert them.
</Info>
