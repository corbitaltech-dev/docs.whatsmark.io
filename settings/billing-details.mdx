## Billing Details

Go to **Settings → Billing Details** to manage the information used on your invoices:

- **Company name** and **company email**
- **GST / tax ID**
- **Country**, **state**, and **zip code**
- **Company address**

<Warning>
Only the workspace **owner** can update Billing Details.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Keep this accurate before your next billing cycle — it's what appears on every invoice you download from [Payments & Invoices](/billing/payments-and-invoices).
</Tip>
