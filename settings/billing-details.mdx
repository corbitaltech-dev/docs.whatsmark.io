Go to **Settings → Billing Details** to manage the company information used in the **Bill To** section of your invoices:

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/billing-details-form.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/settings/billing-details"
  alt="Billing Details form showing company name, email, GST number, country, state, zip code, and address fields"
  caption="This information appears on every invoice generated after you save it."
/>

- **Company Name** and **Company Email**
- **GST Number** — your tax identification number, if applicable.
- **Country** — required, and used to calculate GST on your invoices.
- **State / Province** and **ZIP / Postal Code**
- **Company Address**

<Warning>
These details apply to **upcoming invoices only**. Invoices already generated are not updated retroactively — update this page before your next billing cycle if anything has changed.
</Warning>

<Note>
Only the workspace **owner** can update Billing Details.
</Note>
