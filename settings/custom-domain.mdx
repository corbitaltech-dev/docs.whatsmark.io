Every workspace gets a free subdomain like `acme.whatsmark.io`. If you'd rather your team (and customers, for the public booking link) see your own domain, connect one under **Settings → Custom Domain**.

<Warning>
Custom domains are gated by your plan — check with your workspace admin or [Billing](/billing/plans-and-subscription) if you don't see this option.
</Warning>

## Connecting a domain

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter your domain">
  Type the domain (or subdomain) you want to use, e.g. `app.yourcompany.com`.
</Step>

<Step title="Point DNS at WhatsMark">
  Add a **CNAME** record at your DNS provider pointing your domain to the target WhatsMark provides on this page.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify">
  Click **Verify** once your DNS record is live. WhatsMark checks it and begins provisioning SSL automatically.
</Step>

<Step title="Set as primary (optional)">
  Once verified, you can set the custom domain as your **primary** domain — this is what your workspace's links use by default, in place of your `*.whatsmark.io` subdomain.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
DNS changes can take time to propagate. If verification doesn't succeed immediately, wait a few minutes and try again — SSL provisioning also happens asynchronously, so the page polls for status until it's ready.
</Note>

## Removing a domain

You can remove a connected custom domain at any time — your workspace falls back to its original `*.whatsmark.io` subdomain automatically.
