Every workspace gets a free subdomain like `acme.whatsmark.io`. If you'd rather your team (and customers, for the public booking link) see your own domain, connect one under **Settings → Custom Domain**.

  caption="Enter your domain (e.g. app.yourcompany.com) and click Connect Domain to begin."
/>

<Warning>
Custom Domain is an **add-on**. If it's locked, click it to **Buy Add-on** (or **Upgrade Plan** on Free). Only the workspace **owner** can manage it.
</Warning>

## Connecting a domain

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter your domain">
  Type the domain or subdomain you want to use (e.g. `app.yourcompany.com`), without `http://` or `https://`, and click **Connect Domain**.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a CNAME record">
  At your DNS provider, create a **CNAME** record using the **Name/Host** and **Value/Target** shown under DNS Setup Instructions. Click the copy icon to grab the target exactly.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify">
  Click **Verify**. WhatsMark.io checks the DNS record and, once it resolves, begins provisioning an SSL certificate automatically — this typically takes 1–5 minutes.
</Step>

<Step title="Set as primary (optional)">
  Once the domain shows **Verified**, a **Primary domain** choice appears — pick your custom domain or your `*.whatsmark.io` subdomain as the one your workspace's links use by default.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Using Cloudflare for DNS? Set the CNAME's proxy status to **DNS only** (grey cloud), not **Proxied** (orange cloud). A proxied CNAME hides the real target from WhatsMark.io, which blocks SSL certificate issuance.
</Tip>

<Note>
DNS changes can take a few minutes to propagate. If verification doesn't succeed immediately, wait and click **Retry**.
</Note>

## Removing a domain

Click the trash icon next to your domain to remove it — your workspace falls back to its `*.whatsmark.io` subdomain automatically.

<Note>
If your custom domain is set as the **primary** domain, switch back to the subdomain first — removal is blocked while the custom domain is primary.
</Note>
