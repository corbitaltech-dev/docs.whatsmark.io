A **Custom Domain** lets you access your WhatsMark.io workspace using your business domain instead of the default workspace URL, providing a more professional and branded experience.

**Example**

- Default: `workspace.whatsmark.io`
- Business Domain: `app.yourcompany.com`

Connect your domain under **Settings → Custom Domain**.

<Note>
Custom Domain is available as an **add-on**. If the feature is locked, click it to purchase the add-on or upgrade your plan. Only the workspace **owner** can connect, manage, or remove custom domains.
</Note>

## Connecting a domain

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/custom-domain-form.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/settings/custom-domain"
  alt="Custom Domain form with a domain name field and Connect Domain button"
  caption="Enter your domain (e.g. app.yourcompany.com) and click Connect Domain to begin."
/>

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter your domain">
  Type the domain or subdomain you want to use (e.g. `app.yourcompany.com`), without `http://` or `https://`, and click **Connect Domain**.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a CNAME record">
  At your DNS provider, create a **CNAME** record using the **Name/Host** and **Value/Target** shown under DNS Setup Instructions. Click the copy icon to grab the target exactly.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify">
  Click **Verify**. WhatsMark.io checks the DNS record and, once it resolves, begins provisioning an SSL certificate automatically — this typically takes 1–5 minutes.
</Step>

<Step title="Set as primary (optional)">
  Once the domain shows **Verified**, a **Primary domain** choice appears — pick your custom domain or your original WhatsMark.io subdomain as the one your workspace's links use by default.
</Step>
</Steps>

<BrowserFrame
  src="/settings/img/custom-domain-verified.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/settings/custom-domain"
  alt="Custom Domain page showing a verified domain and a Primary domain selector"
  caption="Once verified, choose which domain — your custom domain or your whatsmark.io subdomain — is the primary one."
/>

<Tip>
Using Cloudflare for DNS? Set the CNAME's proxy status to **DNS only** (grey cloud), not **Proxied** (orange cloud). A proxied CNAME hides the real target from WhatsMark.io, which blocks SSL certificate issuance.
</Tip>

<Note>
DNS changes can take a few minutes to propagate. If verification doesn't succeed immediately, wait and click **Retry**.
</Note>

## Removing a domain

Click the trash icon next to your domain to remove it — your workspace falls back to its original WhatsMark.io subdomain automatically.

<Note>
If your custom domain is set as the **primary** domain, switch back to the subdomain first — removal is blocked while the custom domain is primary.
</Note>
