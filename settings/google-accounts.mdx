If your [automation flows](/automation/chatbot-flows) need to write to a Google Sheet, connect a Google account first from **Settings → Google Accounts**.

<Warning>
Google integrations require the `google_sheet` or `google_calendar` feature on your plan — both are off on Free and available on Professional and Reseller plans. If your plan doesn't include either, this page shows an **Upgrade Plan** prompt instead of the connect flow.
</Warning>

## Connecting an account

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Connect Google Account">
  Starts a standard Google OAuth flow.
</Step>

<Step title="Sign in and grant access">
  Sign into the Google account you want to use and approve the requested permissions.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm it's connected">
  The account appears in your list with its name, email, and connection date, ready to select from a Google Sheets automation node.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Disconnecting

Click **Disconnect** next to an account to remove it. Any flow nodes using that account will stop writing to Google Sheets until you point them at a different connected account.
