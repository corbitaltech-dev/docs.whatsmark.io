If your [automation flows](/automation/chatbot-flows) need to read/write a Google Sheet or create a Google Calendar event, connect a Google account first from **Settings → Google Accounts**.

<Warning>
This feature requires `google_sheet` and/or `google_calendar` on your plan — both are off on Free and available on Professional and Reseller plans.
</Warning>

## Connecting an account

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Connect">
  Starts a standard Google OAuth flow in a popup.
</Step>

<Step title="Sign in and grant access">
  Sign into the Google account you want to use, and approve the requested permissions (Sheets and/or Calendar access).
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm it's connected">
  The account appears in your list, ready to select from any Google Sheets or Google Calendar automation node.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Using a connected account

Once connected, browse or create **Sheets** and **Calendars** directly from the automation builder when configuring a Google Sheets or Google Calendar node — no need to leave the flow builder to set anything up in Google first.

## Disconnecting

Remove a Google account at any time from the same page. Any automation flow nodes referencing it will need to be pointed at a different account afterward.
