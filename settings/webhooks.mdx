**Webhook Forwarding** sends a copy of your incoming WhatsApp events to a URL you control — useful for building your own integrations. WhatsMark.io keeps processing everything as usual; forwarding just adds a second destination.

<Warning>
Webhook Forwarding is included on the **Professional** plan and above. If it's locked, click **Upgrade Plan**. Only the workspace **owner** can configure it.
</Warning>

## Global Webhook Config

Applied to every connected channel unless a channel below has its own override.

- Toggle forwarding **on** using the switch in the card header.
- **Webhook URL** — required once enabled. The endpoint that receives events.
- **Method** — `POST` or `GET`.
- **Signing Secret** — auto-generated; click the refresh icon to rotate it at any time.
- **Events** — required once enabled. A multi-select of Meta's WhatsApp Business event types (Messages, Message Echoes, Message Template Status Update, Phone Number Quality Update, Group Participants Update, and more) — pick only what you need, you don't have to forward everything.

<Tip>
Every forwarded request is signed with `sha256=HMAC(body, secret)` in the **X-Webhook-Signature** header. Verify this on your endpoint to confirm a request genuinely came from WhatsMark.io before trusting its payload.
</Tip>

## Channel overrides

Channels without an override use the global config above. To send a specific WhatsApp channel's events somewhere else:

1. Find the channel under **Channel Overrides** and turn on **Override**.
2. Turn on **Enable forwarding for this channel**.
3. Set its own **Webhook URL**, **Method**, **Signing Secret**, and **Events** — independent of the global config.
4. Click **Save Channel**.

Turning a channel's override off reverts it to the global config; its own saved settings are kept, just unused until you switch it back on.

<Tip>
Regenerating a secret only changes what's used to sign future requests — your URL and event selection keep working unchanged.
</Tip>
