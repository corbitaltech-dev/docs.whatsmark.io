Connect your Shopify store to WhatsMark to automatically send WhatsApp messages to customers on cart abandoned, order placed, order shipped, and COD confirmed events.

## Before you connect

<Info>
Not every shopper can be reached — Shopify only shares a phone number when your checkout is configured to collect one. Shoppers who don't provide a phone number can't be messaged on WhatsApp.
</Info>

## Connecting your Shopify store

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Settings → Shopify">
  Navigate to the Shopify integration settings.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter your store domain">
  Type your Shopify store domain (e.g., `your-store.myshopify.com`).
</Step>

<Step title="Click Connect Shopify">
  You'll be redirected to Shopify to authorize the connection.
</Step>

<Step title="Authorize WhatsMark">
  Review and approve the permissions WhatsMark needs (read orders, customer access, etc.).
</Step>

<Step title="Return to WhatsMark">
  Once authorized, you're connected and can start using Shopify automation.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Supported events

Once connected, you can create automations for:

- **Cart Abandoned**: When a customer abandons their shopping cart
- **Order Placed**: When a new order is created
- **Order Shipped**: When an order ships
- **COD Confirmed**: When cash-on-delivery payment is confirmed

## Managing your connection

- **Disconnect**: Remove the Shopify integration anytime — existing automations won't run but won't be deleted
- **Re-connect**: If you disconnect, you can reconnect your store again
