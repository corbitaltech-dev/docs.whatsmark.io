No message send or receive on your workspace until you connect at least one **Channel**. All of your connected accounts — across both platforms — live on a single page: **Channels**.

<Info>
This page covers what's shared across both platforms. For platform-specific setup, see [Connecting WhatsApp](/core-setup/channels/whatsapp) or [Connecting Messenger](/core-setup/channels/messenger).
</Info>

<Info>
  Before connecting a channel, make sure you have the following ready:

  - A Facebook account.
  - A **Meta Business account** — Business Verification is not mandatory to connect, but it is strongly recommended; without it your account stays on limited messaging tiers.
  - One mobile number for WhatsApp that must be either:
    - **not connected to any WhatsApp account** (fresh number), or
    - **already connected to the WhatsApp Business App**
  - Your **WABA ID**, **Access Token**, **Meta App ID**, and **App Secret** (for manual connections only).
</Info>

## The Channels page

The Channels page lists every messaging account connected to your workspace — WhatsApp Business Accounts (WABAs) and Facebook Pages (Messenger) — in one unified table, with each row showing its phone number or handle, platform, and connection status.

<Warning>
The number of channels you can connect is limited by your plan, and this limit is shared across **both** platforms combined — it's one pool, not a separate quota per platform. Free-plan workspaces can connect up to 2 channels total; Professional plans raise this to 10; Reseller plans are unlimited. Once you hit your limit, connecting a new channel is blocked until you disconnect one or upgrade.
</Warning>

## Connecting a channel

From the Channels page, click **Connect** and choose a platform from the picker:

  <Card title="WhatsApp" icon="whatsapp" href="/core-setup/channels/whatsapp">
    Connect a WhatsApp Business Account via Embedded Signup or manually.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Messenger" icon="facebook-messenger" href="/core-setup/channels/messenger">
    Connect a Facebook Page via Embedded Signup or manually.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
You can connect more than one channel of each platform — for example, several WhatsApp numbers, or multiple Facebook Pages — and manage all of them from the same Channels page.
</Tip>

## Managing a connected channel

Open any channel from the list to:

- **Set it as the default** endpoint for its platform (used when a feature doesn't ask you to pick a channel explicitly).
- **Refresh** its health status and **view webhook subscription status** — re-subscribe if it's ever out of sync.
- **Update missing app credentials**, if Meta requires you to re-enter them.
- **Send a test message** to confirm the connection actually works end-to-end.
- **Disconnect** the whole account, or just a single phone number/endpoint under it.

<Info>
If a platform isn't available on your plan at all, the Channels page still shows it as a locked row with an upgrade prompt, rather than hiding it entirely.
</Info>
