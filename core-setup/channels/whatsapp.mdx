Connect a WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) so you can send and receive WhatsApp messages through WhatsMark.io.

## Before you connect

<Info>
Make sure you have the following ready:

- A Facebook account.
- A **Meta Business account** — Business Verification is not mandatory to connect, but it is strongly recommended; without it your account stays on limited messaging tiers.
- One mobile number for WhatsApp that must be either:
  - **not connected to any WhatsApp account**, or
  - **already connected to the WhatsApp Business App**
- Your **WABA ID**, **Access Token**, **Meta App ID**, and **App Secret** (for manual connections only).
</Info>

## Connecting WhatsApp

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Connect">
  From the **Channels** page, click **Connect** and choose **WhatsApp** from the platform picker.

  <BrowserFrame
    src="/core-setup/channels/img/channels-connect.png"
    url="whatsmark.io/channels"
    alt="Channels page showing a Connect Channel button and a Supported channels panel listing WhatsApp and Messenger, both marked Available"
    caption="Choose Connect Channel, then pick WhatsApp from the supported channels."
  />
</Step>

<Step title="Pick a connection method">
  You'll see two tabs:

  - **Embedded (recommended)** — WhatsMark.io uses Meta's official **Embedded Signup** flow. A Facebook login popup opens; log into your Facebook Business account, choose or create a WhatsApp Business Account and phone number, and confirm. The popup closes automatically once complete.
  - **Manual** — for advanced setups where you already have WhatsApp Business API credentials. Paste in your existing **WABA ID**, **access token**, **Meta App ID**, and **App Secret** directly.
</Step>

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/channels/img/whatsapp-connect-methods.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/channels"
  alt="Connect a WhatsApp Account sheet shown twice: the Embedded Signup tab with a Standard Signup Launch button, and the Manual tab with fields for channel name, Business Account ID, access token, Meta App ID, and App Secret"
  caption="Embedded Signup launches Meta's guided flow; Manual takes your existing API credentials."
/>

<Step title="Confirm it's connected">
  Once connected, the channel appears in your Channels list with an **Active** status, and a webhook subscription is set up automatically so incoming messages reach your workspace.
</Step>
</Steps>

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/channels/img/channels-list-connected.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/channels"
  alt="Channels list showing two connected channels — a WhatsApp channel and a Messenger channel — each with an Active status, the method used to connect it, and the date connected"
  caption="Connected channels appear here with an Active status. The Method column shows whether the channel was connected via Embedded Signup or Manual."
/>

<Note>
Both connection methods can be individually disabled by your plan. If a tab is missing or disable, that method isn't available on your current plan.
</Note>

## Coexistence mode

If enabled on your plan, you can connect a WhatsApp number in **coexistence mode** — this keeps your existing WhatsApp Business app active on your phone alongside the API connection. Chat history and contacts from the app are imported, and messages stay in sync between the app and WhatsMark.io.

- **Quality rating**, review status, and business verification.
- **Conversation analytics** for the account.
- Per-number quality, if you have multiple phone numbers under the same WABA.
