Connect a Facebook Page so you can send and receive Messenger conversations through WhatsMark, right alongside your WhatsApp channels.

## Connecting Messenger

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Connect">
  From the [Channels page](/core-setup/channels/overview), click **Connect** and choose **Messenger** from the platform picker.
</Step>

<Step title="Pick a connection method">
  You'll see two tabs:

  - **Embedded (recommended)** — a one-click Facebook Page login flow. Log into your Facebook account, choose the Page you want to connect, and confirm. The popup closes automatically once complete.
  - **Manual** — paste in an existing Facebook **Page access token** directly, for advanced setups.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm it's connected">
  Once connected, the Page appears in your Channels list with a **Connected** status, and a webhook subscription is set up automatically so incoming Messenger conversations reach your workspace.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Both connection methods can be individually disabled by your plan. If a tab is missing, that method isn't available on your current plan.
</Note>

## Managing a connected Page

Open a connected Messenger channel to:

- **Refresh** its health status and **view webhook subscription status**.
- **Update missing credentials**, if Meta requires you to re-enter them.
- **Disconnect** the Page.

<Tip>
You can connect more than one Facebook Page — manage all of them from the same [Channels page](/core-setup/channels/overview), alongside your WhatsApp channels.
</Tip>
