Connect a Facebook Page so you can send and receive Messenger conversations through WhatsMark.io, right alongside your WhatsApp channels.

## Before you connect

<Tip>
Make sure you have the following ready:

- A Facebook account.
- A Meta Business account — Business Verification is not mandatory to connect, but it is strongly recommended; without it your account stays on limited messaging tiers.
- A Facebook Page that you manage and want to connect to Messenger.
- Your Page ID, Page Access Token, Meta App ID, and Meta App Secret (for manual connections only).
</Tip>

## Connecting Messenger

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Connect">
  From the **Channels** page, click **Connect** and choose **Messenger** from the platform picker.
  
  <BrowserFrame
    src="/core-setup/channels/img/channels-connect.png"
    url="whatsmark.io/channels"
    alt="Channels page showing a Connect Channel button and a Supported channels panel listing WhatsApp and Messenger, both marked Available"
    caption="Image shows the Channels page with the Connect Channel button."
  />
</Step>

<Step title="Pick a connection method">
  You'll see two tabs:

  ![Messenger connect flow showing Embedded Signup and Manual connection options](/core-setup/channels/img/messenger-connect-methods.png)

  - **Embedded Signup** — connect with Meta's secure Facebook login flow.
    - Sign in using your Facebook credentials.
    - Select the Page you want to connect.
    - Confirm the requested permissions.
    - The connection completes automatically and the popup closes when done.
  - **Manual** — use this for advanced setups with existing Meta credentials.
    - Enter the **Page ID**.
    - Paste the **Page Access Token**.
    - Provide the **Meta App ID** and **Meta App Secret**.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm it's connected">
  Once connected, the Page appears in your Channels list marked **Active**, and incoming Messenger conversations start reaching your workspace automatically.
</Step>
</Steps>

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/channels/img/channels-list-connected.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/channels"
  alt="Channels list showing two connected channels — a WhatsApp channel and a Messenger channel — each with an Active status, the method used to connect it, and the date connected"
  caption="Image shows the Channels list with connected channels and their Active status."
/>

<Note>
Both connection methods can be individually disabled by your plan. If a tab is missing or disable, that method isn't available on your current plan.
</Note>

## Managing a connected Page

Open a connected Messenger channel to:

- **Review Page information** fetched from Meta, including Page name, Page ID, category, and connection method.
- **Check** channel health and **view webhook subscription status**.
- **Disconnect** the Page.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/channels/img/messenger-view.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/channels"

/>

<Tip>
You can connect more than one Facebook Page — manage all of them from the same **Channels** page, alongside your WhatsApp channels.
</Tip>
