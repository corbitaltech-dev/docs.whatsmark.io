Groups are named collections of contacts used for manual segmentation and as audience options when creating campaigns or automations.

## Creating groups

Click **Create Group** to open the group form:

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/img/group-form.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/setup/groups"
  alt="New Group form showing fields for name, description, and color"
  caption="Use a clear group name, an optional description, and a badge color."
/>

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Setup → Groups">
  Navigate to the groups section.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Create Group">
  Open the side sheet to add a new contact group.
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in the details">
  Configure the group:

  - **Name** — enter a clear group name, such as **VIP Customers**, **Newsletter Subscribers**, or **Support Requests**.
  - **Description** — optionally add context about who belongs in this group.
  - **Color** — choose the badge color used wherever the group appears.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
  Your group is now created and ready to receive members.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Adding members to groups

- **From the Contacts page**: Select contacts and add them to groups in bulk
- **When creating a contact**: Assign groups during contact creation
- **Edit contact details**: Add or remove groups anytime

## Managing groups

- **Edit** a group to update its name, description, or color.
- **Delete** a group only when it is not being used by contacts or related records.
- The **Contacts** column on the groups table shows how many contacts are currently in each group.

<Tip>
You can also create a new group while adding or editing a contact. Click the `+` icon beside the **Groups** field in the contact form.
</Tip>

## Using groups in campaigns

When you create a [Campaign](/messaging/campaigns), you can select one or more groups as your audience. This lets you target specific segments without manually selecting individual contacts each time.
