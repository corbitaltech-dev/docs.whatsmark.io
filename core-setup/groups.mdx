A **group** lets you organize contacts based on your business needs. For example, you can create groups for VIP Customers, Newsletter Subscribers, or Retail Customers. A contact can be added to multiple groups and selected whenever you send a [campaign](/messaging/campaigns).

## Where groups show up

- **Campaigns** — when creating a campaign, choose a group as your audience and everyone in it receives the message.
- **Contacts page** — filter your list by group, or select many contacts and add them all to a group in one go. See [Bulk Actions & Merge](/contacts/bulk-actions-and-merge).
- **Contact details** — a contact's groups appear in their details panel.
- **New contacts from messages** — when a message from a new person creates a contact, you can have them placed into groups for you. Set them in [Contact Configuration](/settings/contact-configuration).

## Creating a group

![New Group form showing fields for name, description, and color](/core-setup/img/group-form.png)

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Groups">
  Click **Setup** in the sidebar, then **Groups**.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Create Group">
  The button sits at the top right of the page. A form opens on the right side of the screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in the details">
  - **Name** — a clear group name, such as **VIP Customers** or **Newsletter Subscribers**. Each name can be used only once.
  - **Description** — optionally note who belongs in this group, so your team adds the right people.
  - **Color** — the badge color shown wherever the group appears. Click the swatch to pick one, or type a color code.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
  Click **Save**. The group is created and ready to receive contacts.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can also create a group without leaving the contact form — click the `+` icon beside the **Groups** field while adding or editing a contact.
</Tip>

## Adding contacts to a group

- **When creating or editing a contact** — pick one or more groups in the **Groups** field of the contact form.
- **In bulk** — on the Contacts page, select multiple contacts and add them all to a group at once. See [Bulk Actions & Merge](/contacts/bulk-actions-and-merge).
- **Automatically** — have contacts created from incoming messages placed into a group. Set it in [Contact Configuration](/settings/contact-configuration).

## Managing groups

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/img/group-table.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/groups"
  alt="Groups table showing a group with its name, description, color dot, contact count, and a row menu with Edit and Delete"
  caption="Image shows the Groups table with a row menu open."
/>

From the Groups page you can:

- The **Contacts** column shows how many contacts are in each group.
- **Edit** — click the menu icon at the end of a row and click **Edit** to change the name, description, or color.
- **Delete** — click the menu icon and click **Delete**.

<Note>
A group that's still in use can't be deleted. If any contacts are in it, or it's chosen as a default in [Contact Configuration](/settings/contact-configuration), WhatsMark.io tells you where it's used — clear it there first, then delete.
</Note>
