A role is a set of permissions that you assign to team members. Create roles like **Sales Agent**, **Support Agent**, or **Manager**, then assign them to one or more team members. Updating a role automatically updates permissions for everyone using it.

Find it under **Setup → Roles** in the sidebar, or click **Manage Roles** on the Team page.

## Create a role

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Create Role">
  The button sits at the top right. A form opens on the right side of the screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Name the role">
  Use the name of the job, such as **Support Agent** — this is what you will pick from later when adding a team member.
</Step>

<Step title="Check what this role can do">
  Below the name is the permission list. Each row is one part of the product, and the boxes in that row are what the role can do there.

  ![The Create Role form with a role name field and a list of features with permission checkboxes](/core-setup/img/roles-create.png)
</Step>

<Step title="Click Save">
  The role is ready to assign. Head to [Team](/core-setup/team-and-roles) to give it to someone.
</Step>
</Steps>

## How the permissions work

Most rows offer the same four boxes:

- **View** — see the page and what is on it.
- **Create** — add new items.
- **Edit** — change existing items.
- **Delete** — remove items.

<Note>
Selecting any permission automatically selects **View**. This ensures team members can only **create**, **edit**, or manage items they can **view**. If you clear all other permissions, View becomes available to change again.
</Note>

Click the feature name on the left to check or uncheck that whole row at once.

### Rows that work differently

Most rows need no explanation. These few have extra boxes worth knowing about:

| Row | What the extra boxes do |
| --- | --- |
| **Contacts** | **Import** brings contacts in from a file. **Initiate Chat** starts a new conversation from a contact. **View Own** is a narrower View — the member sees only the contacts assigned to them. |
| **Channels** | **Connect** and **Disconnect** replace Create and Delete. |
| **Team** | **Invite** sends an email invitation. **Create** adds a member directly, with a password you set. |
| **Templates** | **Load** pulls your approved templates in from Meta. |
| **WhatsApp Flows** | **Submission View** sees the answers customers filled in on a form. |
| **Invoices** | **Download** saves an invoice as a PDF. |
| **Subscription**, **Billing** | View only. Changing a plan, cancelling, or buying an add-on is always the owner's job. |
| **Chat** | Has no View box — see below. |

### Chat

Chat has no **View** box. A member reaches the inbox if they hold **any** chat permission at all, or if a conversation has been assigned to them.

- **Send Message** — reply in a conversation. Without it, the inbox is read-only.
- **Send Template** — send an approved template.
- **Run Flow** — start an automation or send a form inside a chat.
- **Close** — close the conversation they have open.
- **Clear** — delete every message in a conversation.
- **Delete** — delete a whole conversation.
- **Delete Message** — delete and restore individual messages.

<Note>
Assigning conversations to members or teams is not on this list. Only the admins can do it.
</Note>

## Admins always have full access

Roles apply only to team members. Team members marked as **Is Admin** always have full access, regardless of the role assigned to them.

## Edit or delete a role

Click a role's name to reopen it, or use the row menu for **Edit** and **Delete**. Edits reach everyone holding the role the moment you save.

<Warning>
A role still assigned to someone cannot be deleted. Check the **Members** column, move those people to another role first, then delete it.
</Warning>
