The WhatsApp Activity Log shows every outbound message sent on your behalf through WhatsMark.io — whether sent manually, via automation, or via campaigns. Use it to monitor delivery, troubleshoot failures, and audit your messaging activity.

## Viewing the log

Navigate to **Setup → WhatsApp Activity Log** to view all messages:

  src="/core-setup/img/whatsapp-activity-log.png"

**Columns explained:**

- **Category**: Message type (Bot Flow, Initial Chat, Campaign, Canned Reply, etc.)
- **Name**: Who sent it or what automation triggered it
- **Template Name**: Template used (if applicable)
- **Status**: Delivery status (read, delivered, failed, etc.)
- **Error**: Any failure reason if status is failed
- **Relation Type**: Contact, Lead, or other relationship
- **Created**: Timestamp when the message was sent

## Filtering and searching

- **All/Success/Errors tabs**: Filter by delivery status
- **Search**: Find messages by contact name, template, or other details
- **Filters**: Further narrow results by category, status, or date range

## Understanding message status

- **Read**: Message was delivered and read by the recipient
- **Delivered**: Message was sent and arrived but not yet read
- **Failed**: Message failed to send — check the error column for the reason
- **Pending**: Message is queued and waiting to be sent

## Troubleshooting failed messages

If you see failed messages:

1. Check the **Error** column for the specific failure reason
2. Common errors: invalid phone number, contact not found, template rejected by Meta, network issues
3. Verify the contact details and try sending again if the issue was temporary
4. For template errors, review the template for compliance with Meta's policies

## Common use cases

- **Audit compliance**: Review all messages sent for compliance and legal records
- **Troubleshoot delivery issues**: Identify why messages failed and fix contact data
- **Monitor campaign performance**: Check delivery rates and errors for active campaigns
- **Debug automations**: Verify that your bot flows and automations sent messages as expected
