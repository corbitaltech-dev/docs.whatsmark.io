A status shows the current stage of a contact, such as **New Lead**, **Follow Up**, or **Customer**. Using statuses helps you organize contacts, track their progress, and quickly find the people who need your attention.

WhatsMark.io starts you off with five ready-made statuses — **New**, **In Progress**, **Contacted**, **Qualified**, and **Closed** — with **New** set as the default. Rename them, recolor them, or replace them with stages that match how your business actually works.

## Where statuses show up

Once set, a status follows the contact everywhere:

- **Contacts page** — each contact shows their status as a colored badge, and you can filter the list by status.
- **Chat** — the sidebar lets you filter conversations by contact status, with a count next to each one.
- **Campaigns** — when picking who receives a [campaign](/messaging/campaigns), you can filter the audience by status.
- **Exports** — filter by status before exporting to download just that slice of your list. See [Import & Export](/contacts/import-export).
- **New contacts from messages** — when someone messages you first, WhatsMark.io creates the contact automatically and gives them your chosen default status. You can change these defaults in [Contact Configuration](/settings/contact-configuration).

## Creating a status

![New Status form showing fields for name, color, sort order, and a default status switch](/core-setup/img/status-form.png)

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Statuses">
  Click **Setup** in the sidebar, then **Statuses**.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Create Status">
  The button sits at the top right of the page. A form opens on the right side of the screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in the details">
  - **Name** — a clear stage name, such as **New Lead**, **Quoted**, or **Won**. Required, and each name can be used only once.
  - **Color** — the badge color shown wherever the status appears. Click the swatch to pick one, or type a color code. Leave it empty and WhatsMark.io picks a color for you.
  - **Sort Order** — the position of this status in lists. It's pre-filled with the next spot, so you can usually leave it as is.
  - **Default status** — turn this on to give this status to every new contact automatically. Only one status can be the default, so turning it on here turns it off on the previous one.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
  Click **Save**. The status is ready to use right away in contact forms, filters, chat, and campaigns.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can also create a status without leaving the contact form — click the `+` icon beside the **Status** field while adding or editing a contact.
</Tip>

## Managing statuses

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/img/statuses-table.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/statuses"
  alt="Statuses table listing New, In Progress, Contacted, Qualified, and Closed with color dots, order numbers, a default switch on New, and a row actions menu"
  caption="Drag rows to reorder your statuses, and flip the switch in the Default column to change which one new contacts get."
/>

From the Statuses page you can:

- **Reorder** — drag rows up or down. The same order is used everywhere statuses are listed.
- **Change the default** — flip the switch in the **Default** column. The previous default is turned off automatically.
- **Edit** — click the menu icon at the end of a row and click **Edit** to change the name, color, order, or default setting.
- **Delete** — click the menu icon and click **Delete**.

<Note>
A status that's still in use can't be deleted. If any contacts have it, or it's chosen as a default in [Contact Configuration](/settings/contact-configuration), WhatsMark.io tells you where it's used — clear it there first, then delete.
</Note>
