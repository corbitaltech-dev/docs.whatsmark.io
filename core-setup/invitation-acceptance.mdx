Send an email invitation to team member. The memeber can create their own account and join your workspace with the assigned role.

## Sending an invitation

Click **Invite Member** on the [Team](/core-setup/team-and-roles) page. Enter an **email address** and select a **role**.

![The Invite Team Member form with an email field and a role picker](/core-setup/img/invite-member.png)

<Note>
The invite link works for **7 days**. After that you need to send a new one.
</Note>

## Pending invitations

The Pending Invitations list shows members who have not accepted invitation yet, along with their role and the invitation date.

**Revoke** cancels the invitation, and invitation link stops working right away.

![The Pending Invitations list showing an invited email, its role, when it was sent, and a Revoke button](/core-setup/img/pending-invitation.png)

## What the invited member does

Here is what your team member need to do after you send the invitation.


<Steps>
<Step title="Open the invitation">
  They click **Accept Invitation** in the email, which opens an account creation form.
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in their details">
  ![The accept invitation form where the invited person creates their account](/core-setup/img/accept-invitation.png)
  - **Full name** — their name.
  - **Email address** — already filled in from the invitation.
  - **WhatsApp number** — their own number, with the country code.
  - **Password** and **Confirm password** — at least 8 characters.
</Step>

<Step title="Accept the invitation">
  They click **Accept invitation** to create their account and join with the role you gave them.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify their WhatsApp number">
  Enter a 6-digit code sent to their number.
</Step>

<Step title="Land on the dashboard">
  They are redirect to the Dashboard and can start working.
</Step>
</Steps>
