If someone invites you to their WhatsMark.io workspace, you'll receive an email with a link to an invitation page rather than the normal registration form.

## Accepting your invitation

<BrowserFrame
  src="./img/accept-invitation.png"
  alt="Accept invitation form"
  caption="Create your account form to accept the workspace invitation"
/>

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Accept Invitation">
  In the invitation email, click the **Accept Invitation** button to open the account creation form.
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in your account details">
  Complete the form with the following information:
  - **Full name** — Your complete name
  - **Email address** — Pre-filled from the invitation
  - **WhatsApp number** — Enter with the correct country code. Must be a valid WhatsApp number as it will be used for OTP verification.
  - **Password** — Minimum 8 characters
  - **Confirm password** — Re-enter your password to confirm

<Warning>
Use a unique WhatsApp number. Do not use a number that's already assigned to another team member in the workspace, as each number can only be used for OTP verification once.
</Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Accept the invitation">
  Click the **Accept invitation** button to create your account and join the workspace with the assigned role.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify your WhatsApp number">
  You'll receive a 6-digit verification code on your WhatsApp number. Enter it to complete the verification — the same process used during [registration](/getting-started/register#step-3-verify-your-whatsapp-number).
</Step>

<Step title="Access your workspace">
  Once verified, you're added to the workspace and taken straight to the Dashboard.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
An invitation link can be revoked by the workspace owner or an admin at any time before you accept it. If you see an error saying the invitation is no longer valid, ask whoever invited you to send a new one.
</Warning>
