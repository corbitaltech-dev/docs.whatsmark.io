If someone invites you to their WhatsMark workspace, you'll receive an email with a link to an invitation page rather than the normal registration form.

## Accepting your invitation

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the invitation link">
  The link opens a page showing which workspace you've been invited to and the role you've been assigned.
</Step>

<Step title="Set up your account">
  Create a password for your account, or use a social sign-in option (Google, GitHub, or Facebook) if the workspace has it enabled.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify your WhatsApp number">
  Enter your WhatsApp number and confirm it with a 6-digit code sent to it — the same verification step used during [registration](/getting-started/register#step-3-verify-your-whatsapp-number).
</Step>

<Step title="Join the workspace">
  Once verified, you're added to the workspace with the role the inviter assigned, and taken straight to the Dashboard.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
An invitation link can be revoked by the workspace owner or an admin at any time before you accept it. If you see an error saying the invitation is no longer valid, ask whoever invited you to send a new one.
</Warning>
