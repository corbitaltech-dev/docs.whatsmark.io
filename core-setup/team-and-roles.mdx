At first you are the only person in your workspace. Add everyone else who works with you from this page.


## Adding a team member

Choose one of the following ways to add a team member:

### Create Member

Create the account yourself by entering the team member's details and assigning a role. They can sign in once the account is created.

![The Add Member form with fields for name, email, WhatsApp number and password](/core-setup/img/create-member.png)

First their details:

- **Name** — their full name.
- **Email** — they sign in with this, and it should be unique.
- **WhatsApp number** — should also be unique. Each member needs their own number.
- **Password** and **Confirm Password** — at least 8 characters. Use **Generate** if you would rather not think of one.

Then click **Next** to choose what they can do. Either turn on **Is Admin**, or pick a **Role** from the list. Once you pick a role you can see exactly what it allows. Only the workspace owner can make a member an admin.

### Invite them by email

Invite a member by email so they set up their own account. See [Invite Team Members](/core-setup/invitation-acceptance) to see how it works.

## Additional permissions for one member

You can add extra permissions to a single member without making a new role.

When adding or editing a team member, you can assign additional permissions that are not included in their role. These permissions apply only to that team member.

## Editing and removing members

Click a member's name to open their details, or use the menu at the end of their row. You can change their role at any time.

<Warning>
When you remove a member, their contacts and chats are not given to anyone else. Give their contacts and chats to another member first.
</Warning>

## Team member limits

Your plan decides how many members can be in your workspace. Pending invitations also count.
