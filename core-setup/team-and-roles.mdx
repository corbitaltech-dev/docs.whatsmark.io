Every workspace starts with just its owner. Add team members directly or invite them via email, and create custom roles with granular permissions to control their access.

## Adding team members

There are two ways to add team members to your workspace:

### Method 1: Create Member

<BrowserFrame
  src="./img/create-member.png"
  alt="Add Member dialog"
  caption="Create member with details and role assignment"
/>

Click the **Create Member** button to directly create a team member and assign them a role.


<Steps>
<Step title="Enter member details">
  Fill in the following information:
  - **Full name** — The member's complete name
  - **Email address** — Must be valid as it's used for email verification
  - **WhatsApp number** — Used for OTP verification

<Warning>
Each team member must have a unique WhatsApp number. Do not use the same WhatsApp number for multiple team members as it will be used for OTP verification.
</Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Set a password">
  Create a password (minimum 8 characters) or click **Generate strong password** to create one automatically.
</Step>

<Step title="Assign a role">
  Click **Next** to proceed. Then choose the role for this team member from the Role dropdown.
</Step>

<Step title="Save the member">
  Click **Save** to create the member. They will immediately have access to the workspace with the role you assigned.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: Inviting your team

<BrowserFrame
  src="./img/invite-member.png"
  alt="Invite Team Member dialog"
  caption="Send invitation email to add a new team member"
/>

Send an invitation email to add a new member to your workspace.

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Invite Member">
  Click the **Invite Member** button to open the invitation form.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter email address">
  Enter the email address of the person you want to invite. The email must be valid and accessible so they can receive the invitation.
</Step>

<Step title="Assign a role">
  Select a role from the dropdown menu to assign to this team member. The role controls what features and actions they can access in the workspace.
</Step>

<Step title="Send the invitation">
  Click **Send Invite** to send the invitation email. Pending invitations stay listed until accepted. You can revoke an invitation at any time before it's accepted.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
How many people you can add depends on your plan. **Free** covers just you, the workspace owner, so you'll need to move to **Professional** before inviting anyone. **Professional** includes **5 team members**, and you can add more seats whenever you need them. See [Free vs. Paid Plans](/getting-started/free-vs-paid).
</Note>

<Info>
See how the invitation looks from the team member's perspective. Check [Accepting a Team Invitation](/core-setup/invitation-acceptance).
</Info>

## Roles & Permissions

<BrowserFrame
  src="./img/roles-create.png"
  alt="Create Role dialog"
  caption="Create Role dialog with permission matrix for features like Contacts, Channels, Campaigns, Team, Roles, Automations, and Templates"
/>

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Create Role">
  Click the **Create Role** button to open the role creation form.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter role name">
  Give the role a descriptive name (e.g. "Content Manager", "Sales Agent").
</Step>

<Step title="Set permissions per feature">
  For each feature (Contacts, Channels, Campaigns, Flows, Chat, Billing, Automations, Templates, and more), choose which permissions this role should have:
  - **View** — access to see the feature
  - **Create** — ability to create new items
  - **Edit** — ability to modify existing items
  - **Delete** — ability to remove items
  - Additional actions like **Import**, **Connect**, **Disconnect**, **Invite**, **Load**, etc. based on the feature
</Step>

<Step title="Save the role">
  Click **Save** to create the custom role. It will immediately be available in the role dropdown when inviting or creating team members.
</Step>
</Steps>
