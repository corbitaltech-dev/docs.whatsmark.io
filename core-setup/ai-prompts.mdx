When you are chatting, you can ask AI to rewrite what you typed. It comes with a few built-in options like fixing grammar or making a message shorter. AI Prompts let you add your own.

A prompt is just an instruction you save once, like *rewrite this in a warm sales tone*. The AI gets the message you typed plus your instruction, and gives you a new version of it.

## What you see on the screen

The page lists every prompt you have saved:

- **Name** — what agents see in the AI menu.
- **Prompt** — the first part of your instruction.
- **Active** — a switch that turns the prompt on or off.
- **Order** — where it sits in the AI menu.
- **Created** — when it was added.

Use the search box to find a prompt by its name or its instruction.

![The AI Prompts page listing saved prompts with drag handles, name, instruction preview, an Active switch, order number and created date, with a search box and a Create AI prompt button](/core-setup/img/ai-prompt.png)

## Creating a prompt

Click **Create AI prompt**. A form opens on the right with two fields.

![The new AI prompt form with a name field and a prompt instruction field](/core-setup/img/ai-prompt-form.png)

- **Name** — what agents see in the AI menu, like *Friendly sales tone*. Up to 100 characters, and it has to be different from your other prompts.
- **Prompt** — the instruction itself. Describe how the AI should change the message. Up to 4000 characters.

New prompts are switched on straight away.

<Tip>
Be specific about what you want. *Make it better* gives you random results, but *rewrite this in a warm, friendly tone and keep it under two sentences* gives you the same kind of answer every time.
</Tip>

### Some prompts to start with

- **Make it friendlier** — *Rewrite this in a warm, conversational tone. Keep it short.*
- **Sales tone** — *Rewrite this to be more persuasive and end with a clear next step.*
- **Explain simply** — *Rewrite this so someone with no technical knowledge can understand it.*
- **Add emojis** — *Rewrite this with a few relevant emojis so it feels friendly.*

## Using a prompt in a chat

Type your message first. The AI works on what you have written, so an empty box gives it nothing to do.

Then click the **sparkle** icon under the message box. Your prompts are listed under **Your prompts**, below the built-in options.

![The AI menu in the chat message box showing built-in quick actions and your own saved prompts](/core-setup/img/use-ai-prompt.png)

Pick one and your message is replaced with the rewritten version. A small bar appears above the box with two options:

- **Undo** — put your original message back.
- **Regenerate** — try again for a different version.

Nothing is sent automatically, so you can still edit it before you press Send.

## Turning prompts on and off

The **Active** switch is in the list, on the prompt's row. Turn it off and the prompt disappears from the AI menu in chat, but you keep it here for later.

Drag rows up or down to change their order. That is the order agents see them in the AI menu.

To delete a prompt, use the menu at the end of its row. You are asked to confirm, because agents will no longer see it in chat.
