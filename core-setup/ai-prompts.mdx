AI Prompts are custom rewrite presets to apply to messages from the chat composer's AI menu. For example, convert a reply into friendlier language, convert to sales language, or rewrite for a specific tone.

## Creating AI prompts

Click **Create AI Prompt** to open the prompt form:

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/img/ai-prompt.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/setup/ai-prompts"
  alt="New AI Prompt form showing fields for name and prompt instruction"
  caption="Write clear, specific instructions so the AI rewrites messages exactly how you want."
/>

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Setup → AI Prompts">
  Navigate to the AI prompts section.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Create AI Prompt">
  Enter a descriptive name (e.g., "Make it friendlier", "Convert to sales pitch").
</Step>

<Step title="Write the prompt instruction">
  Describe exactly how the AI should rewrite the message. Be specific about tone, style, length, or any other requirements.
</Step>

<Step title="Choose active status">
  Toggle to enable or disable the prompt. Inactive prompts won't appear in the AI menu.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
  Your AI prompt is ready to use.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Using AI prompts in chat

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/img/use-ai-prompt.png.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/chat"
  alt="AI menu in chat composer showing quick actions and custom prompts"
  caption="Access AI prompts from the chat composer to rewrite messages instantly."
/>

<Note>
Write your message first in the composer, then click the sparkles icon to apply a prompt and rewrite it instantly.
</Note>

When composing a message:

- Click the **sparkles icon** in the chat composer to start the AI menu
- Select one of your prompts
- The AI rewrites the message according to your instruction
- Edit as needed and send

## Managing AI prompts

- **Edit** prompt instructions to refine the results
- **Reorder** by dragging — the order here is the order they appear in the AI menu
- **Deactivate** a prompt without deleting it — just toggle it off

<Tip>
You can also create a new AI prompt on the fly from the chat composer. Click the sparkles icon, then **Manage prompts** to jump to the setup page and create one without leaving the chat.
</Tip>

## Common AI prompt examples

Here are some prompts you can create:

- **"Make it friendlier"** — Rewrite in a warm, conversational tone.
- **"Convert to sales language"** — Rewrite to be more persuasive and action-oriented.
- **"Shorten it"** — Rewrite in one sentence with only essential information.
- **"Add emojis"** — Rewrite with relevant emojis to feel more engaging.
