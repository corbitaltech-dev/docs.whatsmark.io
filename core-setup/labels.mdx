Labels are freeform tags you can attach to any contact to organize and categorize them. For example, "VIP", "Needs follow-up", "Premium customer", etc.

## Creating labels

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Setup → Labels">
  Navigate to the labels section.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Add Label">
  Enter a name for your label.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
  Your label is now available to assign to contacts.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Managing labels

- **Edit** a label name anytime
- **Delete** a label — removing it from all contacts where it's assigned
- **Reorder** by dragging — the order here is the order they appear throughout the product
