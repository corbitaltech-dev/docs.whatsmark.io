Beyond connecting channels and inviting your team, a handful of workspace-wide settings shape how WhatsMark looks and behaves for everyone in your workspace. These live under **Settings**.

## General

Go to **Settings → General** to manage:

- **Workspace name** — shown throughout the dashboard and in emails.
- **Subdomain** and **custom domain** — read-only here; see [Custom Domain](/settings/custom-domain) to add your own domain.
- **Timezone** — used across the dashboard, charts, and scheduling.
- **Date format** and **time format** (12-hour / 24-hour) — controls how dates and times are displayed everywhere in the product.

<Note>
If your workspace is scheduled for deletion (see [Account & Danger Zone](/settings/account-and-danger-zone)), this page also shows the scheduled deletion date.
</Note>

## Business Hours

Go to **Settings → Business Hours** to define:

- **Weekly hours** — open/close time for each day of the week.
- **Holidays / closed dates** — specific dates marked as fully closed regardless of the weekly schedule.

<Info>
Business hours are a shared source of truth used by [Appointments](/automation/appointments) today, and by future features like out-of-hours auto-replies. Set this once and every feature that needs it reads the same schedule.
</Info>

## Billing Details

Go to **Settings → Billing Details** to manage the information used on your invoices:

- **Company name** and **company email**
- **GST / tax ID**
- **Country**, **state**, and **zip code**
- **Company address**

<Warning>
Only the workspace **owner** can update Billing Details.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Keep this accurate before your next billing cycle — it's what appears on every invoice you download from [Payments & Invoices](/billing/payments-and-invoices).
</Tip>
