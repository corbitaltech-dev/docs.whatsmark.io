Sources track where a contact came from. For example, "Website", "Referral", "Ad Campaign", "Direct message", etc. This helps you understand which channels are most effective.

## Creating sources

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Setup → Sources">
  Navigate to the sources section.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Add Source">
  Enter a name for your source (e.g. "Facebook", "Google Ads", "Organic search").
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
  Your source is now available.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Managing sources

- **Edit** a source name
- **Delete** a source — contacts keep their source data but can't be assigned this source going forward
- **Reorder** by dragging — the order here is the order they appear throughout the product

## Using sources in analytics

Your sources appear in dashboard charts and breakdowns, helping you analyze which channels bring the most contacts and which are most valuable for your business.
