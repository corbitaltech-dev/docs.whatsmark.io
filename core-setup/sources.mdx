A source records where a contact came from, such as **Facebook Ads**, **Website**, or **Referral**. When every contact carries a source, you can see which places bring you the most customers and focus your effort there.

WhatsMark.io starts you off with two built-in sources — **WhatsApp** and **Facebook**. When a new person messages you, WhatsMark.io saves them as a contact and sets their source to WhatsApp or Facebook automatically, so you always know they reached out first. These two sources are marked **System** in the table and can't be edited or deleted. Add your own sources for everything else.

## Where sources show up

Once set, a source follows the contact everywhere:

- **Contacts page** — filter your list by source to see everyone from one place.
- **Campaigns** — when picking who receives a [campaign](/messaging/campaigns), you can filter the audience by source.
- **Imports** — when [importing contacts](/contacts/import-export), pick one source to apply to every contact in the file.
- **Exports** — filter by source first, then export to download only those contacts.
- **New contacts from messages** — when a message from a new person creates a contact, the source is set for you. You can change these defaults in [Contact Configuration](/settings/contact-configuration).

## Creating a source

![New Source form showing a required name field](/core-setup/img/source-form.png)

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Sources">
  Click **Setup** in the sidebar, then **Sources**.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Create Source">
  The button sits at the top right of the page. A form opens on the right side of the screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter the name">
  Add a clear name, such as **Facebook Ads**, **Website**, **Walk-in**, or **Referral**. Each name can be used only once.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
  Click **Save**. The source is ready to use right away in contact forms, filters, imports, and campaigns.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can also create a source without leaving the contact form — click the `+` icon beside the **Source** field while adding or editing a contact.
</Tip>

## Managing sources

<BrowserFrame
  src="/core-setup/img/sources-table.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/sources"
  alt="Sources table listing Facebook and WhatsApp, each marked with a System badge, with a Create Source button at the top right"
  caption="Image shows the Sources table."
/>

From the Sources page you can:

- **Reorder** — drag rows up or down. The same order is used everywhere sources are listed.
- **Edit** — click the menu icon at the end of a row and click **Edit** to change the name.
- **Delete** — click the menu icon and click **Delete**.

<Note>
A source that's still in use can't be deleted. If any contacts have it, or it's chosen as a default in [Contact Configuration](/settings/contact-configuration), WhatsMark.io tells you where it's used — clear it there first, then delete. The built-in **System** sources can never be edited or deleted.
</Note>

<Tip>
Stick to one name per place — **Facebook Ads**, not a mix of "FB", "Facebook", and "fb ads". When everyone uses the same names, your filters show the full picture instead of splitting one source into three.
</Tip>
