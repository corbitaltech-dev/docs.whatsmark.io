WhatsMark supports online and offline payment, coupon codes at checkout, and a full invoice history — all from **Billing**.

## Paying online

Online payments cover subscribing to a plan, changing plans, renewals, and add-on purchases. The payment gateway you're offered depends on your billing currency:

- **INR** — pay by card, UPI, netbanking, or wallet through **Razorpay**.
- **USD** — pay through **Dodo Payments**.

Checkout shows a live total that updates instantly when you apply a coupon.

<Note>
Prices are shown **before tax**. Any tax that applies is added on top and shown as its own line at checkout and on your invoice — so the final amount charged is higher than the plan or add-on price on its own.
</Note>

<Note>
Paying in **USD**? Dodo Payments handles tax as merchant of record, so it's calculated when Dodo takes payment rather than previewed beforehand. Your total at Dodo's checkout — and your invoice — show the exact amount charged, tax included.
</Note>

## Paying offline

If your workspace has offline payment enabled, you can instead:

<Steps>
<Step title="View bank details">
  See the bank account (or UPI ID) to transfer payment to, along with any instructions your workspace admin has provided.
</Step>

<Step title="Submit proof">
  Upload proof of your bank transfer.
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for review">
  Your request shows as **Pending** while an admin reviews it — access stays locked behind this screen until it's approved. Your workspace shows the turnaround time to expect (typically a couple of days).
</Step>
</Steps>

Once reviewed, your request shows as either **Approved** — your plan or add-on activates — or **Rejected**, with the admin's reason shown alongside it so you can correct the problem and submit again.

<Note>
Offline payment is available for plan subscriptions, plan changes, and add-on/contact-limit purchases alike — not just initial signup.
</Note>

<Warning>
While an offline payment is **under review**, you can't change your plan. Wait for the decision, and if it's rejected you're free to try again.
</Warning>

## Coupons

At checkout — whether subscribing, changing plans, buying an add-on, or checking out a cart — you can apply a coupon code. The total recalculates live so you can see the discount before you confirm payment.

<BrowserFrame
  src="/billing/img/coupon-checkout.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/billing/payment-selection"
  alt="Checkout showing the selected payment method, a Coupon code field with an Apply button, and a summary listing the plan and Total payable"
  caption="Enter a coupon code and choose Apply — the Total payable updates before you continue."
/>

<Note>
Coupons apply to your **current purchase only**. They don't carry over to your next renewal, so a future renewal will be charged at full price.
</Note>

## Payment methods

You can save a payment method for future purchases, set one as your **default**, and remove any you no longer use.

## Invoices

<BrowserFrame
  src="/billing/img/invoice-history.png"
  url="whatsmark.io/billing/invoices"
  alt="Invoice History table listing invoices with invoice number, amount, gateway, reason, Paid status, and date, with a row menu open showing View and Download"
  caption="Each invoice shows what it was for and how it was paid. Use the row menu to view the full breakdown or download a PDF."
/>

Every billed transaction generates an invoice under **Invoices**:

- Browse a table of past invoices.
- **View** an invoice's full breakdown.
- **Download** it as a PDF for your records.

<Note>
Team members need permission to see invoices, and downloading is a separate permission from viewing — so someone may be able to open an invoice without being able to save the PDF.
</Note>

<Warning>
Only the workspace **owner** can pay, apply coupons, or manage payment methods.
</Warning>
