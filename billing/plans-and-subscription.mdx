**Billing** is where you manage your workspace's plan and subscription — always reachable, even if a payment issue is blocking the rest of the product, so you can fix it yourself.

## Viewing your subscription

Go to **Settings → Billing** to see your current plan, usage, and billing cycle at a glance, or open **My Subscription** for the full detail: status, renewal date, and billing cycle.

## Changing your plan

<Steps>
<Step title="Open plan options">
  From Billing, choose to upgrade, downgrade, or otherwise change your plan.
</Step>

<Step title="Preview the change">
  WhatsMark shows a **proration preview** — what you'll be charged or credited for switching mid-cycle — before you confirm anything.
</Step>

<Step title="Choose a billing cycle">
  Plans are available **Monthly**, **Quarterly**, or **Yearly** — you can switch cycles independently of switching plans.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm">
  Complete the change through your chosen payment method.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you schedule a downgrade for the end of your current cycle, you can **cancel the pending downgrade** at any time before it takes effect, keeping your current plan active.
</Note>

## Cancelling or resuming

You can **cancel** your subscription (it typically stays active until the end of the current billing period) or **resume** a cancelled subscription before it lapses. You can also **disable auto-renewal** if you'd rather manage renewals manually.

## Renewing an expired or expiring subscription

If your subscription has expired or is about to, the **Renew** page runs a renewal calculator so you can see exactly what you'll pay before confirming.

<Warning>
Only the workspace **owner** can change plans, cancel, resume, or manage payment methods — these actions aren't available to other team members regardless of their role.
</Warning>
