**Billing** is where you manage your workspace's plan and subscription — always reachable, even if a payment issue is blocking the rest of the product, so you can fix it yourself.

## Viewing your subscription

Go to **Your account menu**(click your name at the bottom of the sidebar) **→ Billing** to see your current plan, usage, and billing cycle at a glance, or open **My Subscription** for the full detail: status, renewal date, and billing cycle.

## Changing your plan

<Steps>
<Step title="Open plan options">
  From Billing, choose to upgrade or otherwise change your plan. Moving from **Free to Professional** is a one-way upgrade — you can't downgrade back from Professional to Free. Once you're on Professional, you're free to upgrade or downgrade your **billing cycle**.
</Step>

<Step title="Preview the change">
  WhatsMark shows a **proration preview** — what you'll be charged or credited for switching mid-cycle — before you confirm anything.
</Step>

<Step title="Choose a billing cycle">
  Plans are available **Monthly**, **Quarterly**, or **Yearly** — you can switch cycles independently of switching plans. A billing cycle change only takes effect from your **next renewal**, not immediately.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm">
  Complete the change through your chosen payment method.
</Step>
</Steps>

  src="/billing/img/subscription-cycle.png"

<Note>
Your **contact limit doesn't reset when you renew**. It's the total number of contacts you can store — not a monthly allowance. If you're storing 1,000 contacts on a 2,000-contact plan, you'll still have 1,000 slots left after renewal. To free up space, delete contacts you no longer need.
</Note>

## Cancelling a scheduled change

Changes that take effect at your **next renewal** — a new billing cycle, a plan downgrade, or a lower contact limit — stay pending until then. You can **cancel a pending change** any time before it takes effect, and you'll simply carry on with your current plan.

## Understanding your Billing Summary

  src="/billing/img/billing-summary.png"

The **Billing Summary** panel breaks down your price — your base plan, your contact limit, and any add-ons — adding up to the **Total** you pay each cycle. Two lines below the total tell you where you stand:

- **Paid this cycle** — what you actually paid for the cycle you're in.
- **Renews** — your next renewal date, and how many days away it is.

## Cancelling or resuming

You can **cancel** your subscription (it typically stays active until the end of the current billing period) or **resume** a cancelled subscription before it lapses. You can also **disable auto-renewal** if you'd rather manage renewals manually.

## Renewing an expired or expiring subscription

If your subscription has expired or is about to, the **Renew** page runs a renewal calculator so you can see exactly what you'll pay before confirming.

<Warning>
Only the workspace **owner** can change plans, cancel, resume, or manage payment methods. Other team members can be given permission to **view** billing, the subscription, and invoices — but not to make changes.
</Warning>
