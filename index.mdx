## What is WhatsMark

WhatsMark (`whatsmark.io`) is a multi-tenant SaaS platform built on top of the WhatsApp Business Platform (and Meta Messenger). Each customer gets their own isolated **workspace** — a subdomain such as `acme.whatsmark.io` — with its own contacts, conversations, templates, campaigns, and team members.

## Core concepts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Workspace (tenant)">
    A workspace is your isolated account on WhatsMark — its own database, its own subdomain (e.g. `acme.whatsmark.io`), and its own users, contacts, channels, and billing. Everything you do happens inside your workspace.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Channel">
    A Channel is a connected messaging account — most commonly a WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) and phone number, connected either through Meta's Embedded Signup or manually. A workspace can connect more than one channel, including Messenger.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Plans">
    WhatsMark offers a **Free** plan (no payment required, available immediately) and paid **Professional** plans (billed monthly, quarterly, or yearly, priced by contact volume). A separate **Reseller** tier is available by invitation and isn't self-serve.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Roles">
    Every workspace has an owner (the person who registered it) and can invite additional team members with different roles and permissions from the Team page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
