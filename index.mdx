## What you can do with WhatsMark.io

### Marketing — reach thousands, prove the ROI

- **[Campaigns](/messaging/campaigns)** — send one message to your whole contact list, or a filtered slice of it. Schedule it for later, pause it mid-send, or resend just to the people it failed for.
- **[Message templates](/messaging/whatsapp-templates)** — build the messages Meta approves, including buttons, images, carousels, and one-time passcodes.
- **[Merge fields](/messaging/merge-fields)** — drop each person's name, order number, or any custom detail into the message so every send reads as personal.
- **[Campaign analytics](/reporting/dashboard-and-usage)** — see what was sent, delivered, read, and replied to.

### Sales — turn conversations into customers

- **[Automation flows](/automation/chatbot-flows)** — build a chatbot that answers questions and captures leads, using a visual builder with no code.
- **[AI features](/automation/ai-features)** — describe the chatbot you want and AI builds the first version for you. AI can also rewrite or translate replies as you type them.
- **[WhatsApp Flows](/automation/whatsapp-flows)** — collect answers in a proper form inside the chat, without sending anyone to a website.
- **[Appointments](/automation/appointments)** — let customers book a slot themselves, based on your business hours.

### Support — one inbox, every channel, in real time

- **[Live chat](/messaging/live-chat)** — a shared inbox your whole team works from. Assign chats to people or teams so nothing gets answered twice.
- **[Canned replies](/core-setup/canned-replies)** — save the answers you send most and insert them in a keystroke.
- **[Contacts](/contacts/managing-contacts)** — keep every customer's details, notes, and history in one place, with [labels, groups](/core-setup/labels), and [custom fields](/core-setup/custom-fields) to organise them.
- **[Media library](/media/media-library)** — reuse images, documents, and videos across messages instead of re-uploading them.

### Platform — build on it, connect anything

These are **Professional** features, along with canned replies. Everything else above works on the Free plan, within its limits.

- **[REST API](/core-setup/api-keys)** — send messages and manage contacts from your own software.
- **[Webhooks](/settings/webhooks)** — forward events to your systems as they happen.
- **[Shopify](/settings/shopify) and [WooCommerce](/settings/woocommerce)** — recover abandoned carts and send order updates automatically.
- **[Custom domain](/settings/custom-domain)** — run the whole platform on your own web address.
